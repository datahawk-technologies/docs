DataHawk connects your Amazon and Walmart accounts, structures your data, and delivers it through dashboards and databases so you can analyse performance, track advertising, manage inventory, and understand your market — all in one place.

This section will get you from first sign-in to your first report. 

<aside>
💡

**Active setup time is around 30 minutes**, plus a 24-hour wait while your data is being processed.

</aside>
# Setup path

**2. Review what you need to have** ready before you begin.

**3. Set up** with our Step-by-step instructions from first sign-in to your first report.
