## 1. Platform fee

A flat base fee representing the number of credits you need for using the database. The standard amount is 20.

## 2. Account Analytics

Based on your data volume:
- **For Seller accounts:** proxied by number of units sold or orders
- **For Vendor accounts:** proxied by the catalog size
- **For Advertising accounts:** proxied by the amount of ad spend

## 3. Digital Shelf Analytics

Based on the number of items you track per marketplace:
<div style={{ marginBottom: '2rem' }}>
| You track | What data you get |
|---|---|
| Product | Listing details, sales ranks, attributes, pricing, offers, Buy Box, rating, and rating count |
| Keyword | Organic ranks and SEO visibility |
| Category | Top 100 products, market share, brand performance |
</div>

<div style={{ marginBottom: '5rem' }}>
<Callout type="info">
  Product tracking does not include keyword/SEO data — that requires separate keyword tracking. Volume discounts apply based on total contract size.
</Callout>
</div>
