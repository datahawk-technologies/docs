DataHawk connects your Amazon and Walmart accounts, structures your data, and delivers it through dashboards and databases so you can analyze performance, track advertising, manage inventory, and understand your market: all in one place.

This section will get you from first sign-in to your first report.

<Callout type="info">
  Active setup time is around 30 minutes, plus a 24-hour wait while your data is being processed.
</Callout>
