title: Advertising Managers
description: The advertising performance questions DataHawk answers for ad managers — ACoS drivers, keyword conversion, budget allocation across sponsored types, and DSP new-to-brand insights.
---


<Accordions>
  <Accordion title="Which campaigns have the highest ACoS and need to be cut or optimised?">
    You're reviewing this month's spend and your overall ACoS is up 8 points. DataHawk's Advertising Dashboard shows you the exact campaigns driving the spike — broken down by campaign type, product, and keyword — so you can pause or adjust bids before more budget is wasted.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What is my total advertising spend as a percentage of all my sales (TACoS)?">
    Your ACoS looks fine at 18%, but you suspect ad spend is a larger share of your business than it appears. DataHawk calculates TACoS across your full revenue base, revealing that advertising is actually consuming 28% of total sales.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Which products are generating ad-attributed revenue, and at what efficiency?">
    You've been running ads across 40 products but want to know which ones are pulling their weight. DataHawk shows ad-attributed revenue and ACoS by ASIN, so you can concentrate budget on products with strong conversion.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How is my advertising performance trending week over week?">
    You changed your bidding strategy two weeks ago and want to know if it's working. DataHawk's week-over-week view shows spend, ACoS, and ROAS trending together — giving you enough signal to know whether to hold the course or revert.
  </Accordion>
</Accordions>

#### Traffic & listings

<Accordions>
  <Accordion title="How are my page views and session conversion rates trending?">
    Traffic is up but orders are flat — something is breaking in the middle. DataHawk surfaces your session conversion rate by ASIN over time, so you can identify which products have a growing traffic gap and prioritize listing improvements.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Am I winning the Buy Box on my key products, and who is winning it when I'm not?">
    DataHawk shows Buy Box ownership percentage over time and flags when you lost it — so you can investigate pricing, fulfillment, or seller competition as the likely cause.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Which products have poor listing quality scores (LQS) that may be hurting conversion?">
    DataHawk's LQS view flags which ASINs are missing images, have thin bullet points, or fall below quality thresholds.
  </Accordion>
</Accordions>

#### Inventory

<Accordions>
  <Accordion title="Which products are at risk of going out of stock based on current sales velocity?">
    DataHawk calculates days of supply for every ASIN based on recent sales velocity.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How many days of supply do I have for each ASIN?">
    Every ASIN shows current FBA stock and days of supply at current run rate — giving you a clear, prioritized list.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What should I reorder now, and how much?">
    DataHawk's reorder recommendations factor in your sales velocity, current stock, and target cover period.
  </Accordion>
</Accordions>
