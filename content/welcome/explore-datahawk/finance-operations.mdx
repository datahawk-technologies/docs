<Accordions>
  <Accordion title="What is our gross profit, operating profit, and net profit by product, brand, or marketplace?">
    DataHawk consolidates revenue, fees, COGS, and ad spend into a single structured P&L — queryable by product, brand, or marketplace.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What is our total Amazon fee burden across the portfolio?">
    DataHawk breaks out every fee type — FBA fulfilment, referral, storage, returns — by ASIN and rolled up to portfolio level.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Which products have the highest return rates, and what is the financial impact?">
    DataHawk shows return rate by ASIN alongside the revenue impact.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How is our profitability trending across different selling periods?">
    DataHawk's P&L trend view overlays profitability across selling periods.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Where are our COGS-to-revenue ratios out of line with targets?">
    DataHawk compares COGS to revenue by product and flags where the ratio sits outside target thresholds.
  </Accordion>
</Accordions>
