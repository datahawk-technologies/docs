Search is where shoppers discover products and where competitors quietly win demand. DataHawk helps you understand how visible your products are, which keywords matter, and where organic and paid performance should improve.

## What you can achieve with DataHawk

- Track organic and sponsored rank movement for the keywords that matter to your catalog.
- Understand your share of voice by keyword, product, brand, and <Term>marketplace</Term>.
- Find high-potential keywords from search volume, keyword suggestions, and ranking data.
- See which competitors are gaining visibility on terms you care about.
- Connect search visibility with listing quality, advertising performance, and sales outcomes.

## Business outcomes

| Outcome | How DataHawk helps |
|---|---|
| Protect priority keywords | Monitor organic and paid placement so you can react when visibility drops |
| Find new growth terms | Use keyword suggestions, lookup, and search volume to discover demand you are not capturing yet |
| Improve SEO and ads together | Compare organic rank, sponsored visibility, search volume, and ad performance in the same workflow |
| Benchmark competitors | See which competing products and brands are moving up across your tracked search terms |

## Where this creates value

Search visibility work often gets split between SEO, advertising, and content teams. DataHawk brings those signals together so you can decide whether the next move is a bid change, a listing update, a new keyword target, or a broader category push.
