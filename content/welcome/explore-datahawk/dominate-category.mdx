DataHawk helps you see your category like a market map, not just a list of products. You can track leaders, challengers, prices, estimated sales, ranking movement, and new entrants so category decisions are based on evidence.

## What you can achieve with DataHawk

- Measure your estimated market share and how it evolves over time.
- Track the top products and brands in any Amazon category.
- Compare competitor sales estimates, price positioning, <Term>BSR</Term> movement, and review momentum.
- Identify emerging brands before they become obvious threats.
- Find category opportunities by demand, competition, pricing, and assortment gaps.

## Business outcomes

| Outcome | How DataHawk helps |
|---|---|
| Understand the market | Market Intelligence views show the top 100 products, leading brands, price distribution, and estimated sales |
| Track competitive movement | Sales estimates and BSR trends show whether competitors are gaining or losing ground |
| Plan category strategy | Use demand, price, ranking, and brand-share data to decide where to defend, invest, or launch |
| Spot new threats early | Monitor rising brands and products before they show up in your internal sales reports |

## Where this creates value

Category growth requires more than your own sales data. DataHawk shows the market around you, so you can explain whether performance changed because your brand moved, competitors moved, or the category itself shifted.
