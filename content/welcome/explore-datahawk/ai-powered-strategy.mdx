DataHawk uses AI to help teams move from dashboards to decisions. Instead of asking analysts to explain every revenue drop, stockout, ranking shift, or campaign spike from scratch, DataHawk helps identify what changed and what to investigate next.

## What you can achieve with DataHawk

- Diagnose revenue, conversion, ranking, inventory, advertising, and <Term>Buy Box</Term> changes faster.
- Ask performance questions in plain language through supported AI workflows.
- Turn marketplace signals into a prioritized list of actions.
- Combine alerts, Sherlock diagnostics, and connected data to understand root causes.
- Bring DataHawk data into AI assistants and automations through <Term>MCP</Term>.

## Business outcomes

| Outcome | How DataHawk helps |
|---|---|
| Shorten analysis cycles | AI-assisted diagnostics point teams toward the likely drivers of performance changes |
| Prioritize work | DataHawk highlights the products, keywords, accounts, or issues with the clearest business impact |
| Scale expertise | Teams can ask natural-language questions instead of waiting for every custom analysis |
| Automate reporting | MCP connects DataHawk data to AI assistants, workflow tools, and recurring summaries |

## Where this creates value

AI is most useful when it is grounded in clean, current business data. DataHawk gives AI workflows direct access to structured marketplace, advertising, finance, inventory, search, and product data, so recommendations are tied to the performance signals your team already trusts.
