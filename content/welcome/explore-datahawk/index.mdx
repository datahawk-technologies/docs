Explore DataHawk by the business questions your team needs to answer. These role-based paths show how connected marketplace, advertising, product, finance, and market data become practical reporting workflows.

## Key Features

- **Data Sources**: Access product, keyword, market and account data from platforms like Seller Central, Vendor Central, and Advertising for Amazon and Walmart.
- **Modeled & Curated Data**: Our internal semantic layer handles the complexity of data collection, augmentation, and structuring.
- **Programmatic Delivery**: We host your curated data in industry-leading databases (Snowflake, BigQuery, and Databricks).
- **Visualizations & Analytics**: Leverage our Dashboard Templates and Alerting Tools to generate high-quality outputs from your data inputs.

## What can DataHawk answer?

DataHawk connects to your Amazon and Walmart accounts and structures your data so you can answer the questions that matter to your business — without building a data pipeline or pulling reports manually.

## What the output looks like

DataHawk delivers answers through two types of output — you can use one or both:

- **Pre-built dashboards** — Power BI and Looker Studio templates that are ready to use the moment your data is connected. No setup required beyond connecting your accounts.
- **A structured database** — Your data delivered to BigQuery or Snowflake, ready for your own BI tools, custom reports, or data science workflows.
