#### Sales & revenue

<Accordions>
  <Accordion title="Why did my revenue drop this week, and which products drove it?">
    You open your dashboard on Monday morning and revenue is down 18% week over week. DataHawk's Seller Analytics Dashboard shows you immediately that three ASINs account for 80% of the drop — two lost the Buy Box over the weekend, and one had a sharp decline in sessions. You know exactly where to focus before the day starts.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How does my sales performance compare to the same period last year?">
    Before your quarterly review, you pull up the year-over-year comparison in DataHawk. Revenue is up 12% overall, but two of your top SKUs are flat while a newer product is driving most of the growth — insight that changes how you present the story to your leadership team.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What is my average selling price and order value, and how are they trending?">
    You recently ran a promotional discount and want to know if it moved volume without eroding too much margin. DataHawk shows your ASP trending down 6% over the promotion period alongside a 22% uplift in units — giving you the data to decide whether the trade-off was worth it.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Which products are my top and bottom revenue contributors this month?">
    You manage a catalogue of 80 SKUs and need to decide where to invest in restocking. DataHawk ranks every product by revenue contribution and flags which are trending up versus down — so your restock decisions are based on data, not gut feel.
  </Accordion>
</Accordions>

#### Profitability

<Accordions>
  <Accordion title="What is my actual profit after Amazon fees, advertising spend, and returns?">
    Your revenue looks healthy, but you suspect fees are eating into margins. DataHawk's P&L view combines your Amazon fees, ad spend, returns, and your uploaded COGS into a single profit view — showing you that your net margin is 11%, not the 22% gross revenue implied.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How much of my revenue is consumed by FBA fulfilment and referral fees?">
    You're comparing FBA versus FBM for a high-volume product. DataHawk breaks out every fee category by ASIN, so you can see the exact fee burden per unit and run a proper comparison before making the switch.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Which products have the healthiest and weakest margins?">
    Before planning your next advertising push, you check which products actually have margin to absorb ad spend. DataHawk surfaces your top and bottom performers by net margin — so you invest behind products that can generate real profit, not just revenue.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How has my net profit margin evolved over the last 12 months?">
    You want to show investors that the business is becoming more efficient. DataHawk's trend view shows net margin month by month, so you can see the impact of fee changes, pricing adjustments, and cost improvements over time.
  </Accordion>
</Accordions>
