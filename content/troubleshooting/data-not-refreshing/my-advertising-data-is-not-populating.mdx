---
title: My advertising data is not populating
description: Steps to troubleshoot missing advertising data in DataHawk after connecting your Amazon Advertising account.
---

After connecting your Amazon Advertising account to DataHawk, you notice that some or all advertising data is not appearing.

## What I can observe

- No advertising data appears in DataHawk after connection
- Dashboards remain empty or show incomplete data
- Specific campaign types are missing (Sponsored Products, Sponsored Brands, or Sponsored Display)
- You expect data but nothing is retrieved

## Why is this happening?

This is usually due to **campaign status, data availability on Amazon's side, or API sync timing** — not a problem with the DataHawk connection itself.

## What can I do?

### Step 1: Verify campaigns are active

Before investigating further, confirm that your campaigns are not paused or archived.

- Log in to **Amazon Advertising Console**
- Navigate to **Campaign Manager**
- Check the status of your campaigns
  - If campaigns are **Paused**, **Archived**, or **Ended**, no new data will be generated
  - Ensure at least some campaigns are in **Enabled** or **Running** status

<Callout type="info">
  **Note:** Paused campaigns will not generate new data. Historical data from when the campaign was active should still be available, but no new metrics will appear.
</Callout>

### Step 2: Check data availability on Amazon's side

Confirm that data exists in Amazon's native reporting.

- Log in to **Amazon Advertising Console**
- Go to **Reports → Sponsored Ads Reports**
- Download a report for the relevant **date range** and **campaign type**
- Verify that metrics appear in the downloaded file

If the Amazon report is **empty or shows no data**, the issue originates from Amazon — not DataHawk.

### Step 3: Wait for sync completion

DataHawk syncs advertising data on a regular schedule. After connecting a new account or after Amazon processes new campaign data:

- Allow **24–48 hours** for initial data to appear
- Check back to confirm data is populating

## Still not working?

If data is available on Amazon's side but not appearing in DataHawk, please contact DataHawk support and provide the following information:

**Account Identification:**
- DataHawk Account ID or Workspace Name
- Email address used for the connection

**Account Type:** Is this a Seller or Vendor advertising account?

**Marketplace:** Which marketplace(s) are affected? (e.g., US, UK, DE, FR, etc.)

**Campaign Type:** Which campaign type(s) are not showing data?
- Sponsored Products
- Sponsored Brands
- Sponsored Display

<Callout type="info">
  **When contacting support,** please attach a screenshot of the Amazon Advertising report showing that data exists for the affected period. This helps us investigate faster.
</Callout>

## What DataHawk handles

DataHawk automatically:

- Retrieves advertising data via Amazon Advertising API
- Syncs campaign, ad group, keyword, and targeting data
- Handles API requests and rate limits

If no data is returned after completing the checks above, the issue may be related to **API access, account permissions, or Amazon-side data availability**, which requires further investigation.
