---
title: I don't see traffic data for my ASIN
description: How to investigate missing traffic data for an ASIN in DataHawk; checking account type, data source, and API coverage.
---

If traffic data (sessions, page views, Buy Box %) is not appearing for your ASIN in DataHawk, this may be related to how your account type is classified by Amazon.

## What to check

1. Copy the Entity ID or profile identifier shown by DataHawk App
2. Confirm whether your account is classified as Seller or Vendor in Amazon's Advertising API
3. Verify that your Seller Central account is connected and syncing: Traffic data requires a Seller Central connection

**Note:** Traffic data (Glance Views) for Vendor accounts requires that the ASIN is visible in Manufacturing view, not just Sourcing view. If your ASIN is Sourcing-only, traffic data may not be available.

See [I cannot see data in manufacturing views, but I see it in sourcing](/troubleshooting/data-discrepancies/manufacturing-vs-sourcing-views) for more details.

## If your account is correctly connected

- Allow up to 24 hours for data to sync after connecting
- Verify that the ASIN is tracked in your workspace
- Check that the date range selected includes days after the account was connected

## Still not seeing data?

Contact [support@datahawk.co](mailto:support@datahawk.co) with:

- The ASIN(s) affected
- Your marketplace
- The account type (Seller / Vendor)
- Screenshot of what you see in DataHawk
