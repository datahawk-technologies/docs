---
title: Manage your Account
description: Learn how to update your account name, email, password, delete your account, and download your personal data.
---


Go on your DataHawk WebApp \> Settings \> Account

<img
  src="/images/help-center/datahawk-app/account-management/account-management-1.gif"
  alt="Account Management 1"
  width={1200}
  height={680}
  className="rounded-lg border"
/>

## Update your account name

- Select `Update name`

<img
  src="/images/help-center/datahawk-app/account-management/account-management-2.png"
  alt="Account Management 2"
  width={600}
  height={340}
  className="rounded-lg border"
/>

- You will see a pop up where you can change your **first name** and **last name**

<img
  src="/images/help-center/datahawk-app/account-management/account-management-3.png"
  alt="Account Management 3"
  width={600}
  height={340}
  className="rounded-lg border"
/>

- Select `Save` to terminate your update

## Update your email

- Select `Update email`

<img
  src="/images/help-center/datahawk-app/account-management/account-management-4.png"
  alt="Account Management 4"
  width={600}
  height={340}
  className="rounded-lg border"
/>

- You will see a pop up where you can change your **email**

<img
  src="/images/help-center/datahawk-app/account-management/account-management-5.png"
  alt="Account Management 5"
  width={600}
  height={340}
  className="rounded-lg border"
/>

- Select `Save` to terminate your update

## Change your password

- Select `Change password`

<img
  src="/images/help-center/datahawk-app/account-management/account-management-6.png"
  alt="Account Management 6"
  width={600}
  height={340}
  className="rounded-lg border"
/>

- You will see a pop up

<img
  src="/images/help-center/datahawk-app/account-management/account-management-7.png"
  alt="Account Management 7"
  width={600}
  height={340}
  className="rounded-lg border"
/>

- Insert your **current password**
- Insert your **new password**: at least 6 characters, one uppercase character, one lowercase, and one number.
- Select `Save` to terminate your update

## Delete your account

- Select `Delete Account`

<img
  src="/images/help-center/datahawk-app/account-management/account-management-8.png"
  alt="Account Management 8"
  width={600}
  height={340}
  className="rounded-lg border"
/>

- You will see a pop up asking if you are sure to delete the account

<img
  src="/images/help-center/datahawk-app/account-management/account-management-9.png"
  alt="Account Management 9"
  width={600}
  height={340}
  className="rounded-lg border"
/>

- Select `Yes` to confirm. Be aware, any canceled account cannot be recovered.

## Download your data

- Select `More`

<img
  src="/images/help-center/datahawk-app/account-management/account-management-10.png"
  alt="Account Management 10"
  width={600}
  height={340}
  className="rounded-lg border"
/>

- Select `Download My Personal Data`
- An Excel file will download automatically
- In this file you will find the following information:
  - Export Date (UTC)
  - Account Creation Date
  - Email
  - Last name
  - First name
  - Full name
