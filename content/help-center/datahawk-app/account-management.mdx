---
title: Manage your Account
description: Update your account name, email, password, delete your account, or download your personal data.
---

Your DataHawk account is separate from any workspace you belong to — it holds your name, email, password, and personal data. Manage all of it from **Settings → Account** in the web app.

<img
  src="/images/help-center/datahawk-app/account-management/account-management-1.gif"
  alt="Account Management overview"
  width={1200}
  height={680}
  className="rounded-lg border"
/>

## Update your account name

<Steps>

<Step>
### Open the name editor

Click **Update name** in your account settings.

<img
  src="/images/help-center/datahawk-app/account-management/account-management-2.png"
  alt="Click Update name"
  width={600}
  height={340}
  className="rounded-lg border"
/>
</Step>

<Step>
### Edit your first and last name

A pop-up opens where you can change your **first name** and **last name**.

<img
  src="/images/help-center/datahawk-app/account-management/account-management-3.png"
  alt="Edit first and last name"
  width={600}
  height={340}
  className="rounded-lg border"
/>
</Step>

<Step>
### Save

Click **Save** to apply the change.
</Step>

</Steps>

## Delete your account

<Callout type="warn">
  Deleting your account is permanent. We cannot recover a canceled account.
</Callout>

<Steps>

<Step>
### Open the deletion flow

Click **Delete Account**.

<img
  src="/images/help-center/datahawk-app/account-management/account-management-8.png"
  alt="Click Delete Account"
  width={600}
  height={340}
  className="rounded-lg border"
/>
</Step>

<Step>
### Confirm

A confirmation pop-up appears. Click **Yes** to permanently delete your account.

<img
  src="/images/help-center/datahawk-app/account-management/account-management-9.png"
  alt="Confirm deletion"
  width={600}
  height={340}
  className="rounded-lg border"
/>
</Step>

</Steps>

## Download your data

<Steps>

<Step>
### Open the More menu

Click **More**.

<img
  src="/images/help-center/datahawk-app/account-management/account-management-10.png"
  alt="Click More"
  width={600}
  height={340}
  className="rounded-lg border"
/>
</Step>

<Step>
### Trigger the export

Click **Download My Personal Data**. An Excel file downloads automatically.
</Step>

</Steps>

The exported file contains:

- Export Date (UTC)
- Account Creation Date
- Email
- Last name
- First name
- Full name
