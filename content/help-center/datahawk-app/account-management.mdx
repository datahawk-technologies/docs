In this section you will learn how to:

Go on your DataHawk WebApp \> Settings \> Account

{/* TODO: Replace this Notion image with a local copy in /public/images/datahawk-app/ */}

## Update your account name

- Select `Update name`

{/* TODO: Replace this Notion image with a local copy in /public/images/datahawk-app/ */}

- You will see a pop up where you can change your **first name** and **last name**

{/* TODO: Replace this Notion image with a local copy in /public/images/datahawk-app/ */}

- Select `Save` to terminate your update

## Update your email

- Select `Update email`

{/* TODO: Replace this Notion image with a local copy in /public/images/datahawk-app/ */}

- You will see a pop up where you can change your **email**

{/* TODO: Replace this Notion image with a local copy in /public/images/datahawk-app/ */}

- Select `Save` to terminate your update

## Change your password

- Select `Change password`

{/* TODO: Replace this Notion image with a local copy in /public/images/datahawk-app/ */}

- You will see a pop up

{/* TODO: Replace this Notion image with a local copy in /public/images/datahawk-app/ */}

- Insert your **current password**
- Insert your **new password**: at least 6 characters, one uppercase character, one lowercase, and one number.
- Select `Save` to terminate your update

## Delete your account

- Select `Delete Account`

{/* TODO: Replace this Notion image with a local copy in /public/images/datahawk-app/ */}

- You will see a pop up asking if you are sure to delete the account

{/* TODO: Replace this Notion image with a local copy in /public/images/datahawk-app/ */}

- Select `Yes` to confirm. Be aware, any canceled account cannot be recovered.

## Download your data

- Select `More`

{/* TODO: Replace this Notion image with a local copy in /public/images/datahawk-app/ */}

- Select `Download My Personal Data`
- An Excel file will download automatically
- In this file you will find the following information:
  - Export Date (UTC)
  - Account Creation Date
  - Email
  - Last name
  - First name
  - Full name
