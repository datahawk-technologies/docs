---
title: Stripe Subscription Management
description: View and manage your DataHawk subscription, billing information, payment methods, and invoices via the Stripe portal.
---

<Callout type="warn">
  This page applies only to customers with a Stripe subscription. Enterprise customers with a custom contract manage billing directly with our team.
</Callout>

DataHawk uses Stripe to handle subscriptions, invoices, and payments. From **Settings → Manage Subscription** in the web app, you're redirected to the Stripe customer portal where you can review billing details, update payment methods, and pay outstanding invoices.

## Subscription payment status

In the Stripe portal you'll see:

- Whether you have an outstanding payment
- Since when the payment has been outstanding
- The amount of the outstanding payment

  src="/images/help-center/datahawk-app/subscription-management/suscription-management-2.png"

## View invoice history

Scroll down in the Stripe portal to see your full invoice history. Click any invoice row to view or download its details.

## Update your payment method

<Steps>

<Step>
### Locate the payment-method section

Your current payment method is displayed in the Stripe portal.
</Step>

<Step>
### Add or update

Click **+ Add payment method** or **Update Payment Method** to make changes.

<img
  src="/images/help-center/datahawk-app/subscription-management/suscription-management-4.png"
  alt="Update payment method"
  width={300}
  height={170}
  className="rounded-lg border"
/>
</Step>

</Steps>

## Update billing information

<Steps>

<Step>
### Locate the billing-information section

Your current billing information is displayed in the Stripe portal.
</Step>

<Step>
### Edit

## Pay a pending invoice

<Steps>

<Step>
### Click Pay invoice

From the invoice history, click **Pay invoice** on the outstanding row.

<img
  src="/images/help-center/datahawk-app/subscription-management/suscription-management-6.png"
  alt="Click Pay invoice"
  width={300}
  height={170}
  className="rounded-lg border"
/>
</Step>

<Step>
### Confirm payment on the next screen

Stripe takes you to a confirmation page where you can review and confirm the payment.

<img
  src="/images/help-center/datahawk-app/subscription-management/suscription-management-7.png"
  alt="Payment confirmation"
  width={300}
  height={170}
  className="rounded-lg border"
/>
</Step>

<Step>
### Download the invoice

After confirming, you can download a copy of the invoice for your records.
</Step>

</Steps>
