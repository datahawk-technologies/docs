A few moves repeat everywhere in DataHawk — switching <Term>workspace</Term>, using global search, and jumping between the Analyze, Apps, and Sources sections. Get comfortable with them first, then follow the cards below to the dedicated pages for the area you need.

## General across platform
- **Change <Term>workspace</Term>:** Click on your current workspace to see the list and select the next workspace you want to investigate
