In one workspace, you can have several users. You can manage access to the workspace in this section.

Go on your DataHawk WebApp \> Settings \> Members

## Invite members

Add members of your team to the workspace to help you out.

- Click on `+Invite member`
- A pop up shows
- Insert the email address of your additional member

<img
  src="/images/help-center/datahawk-app/members-management/members-management-2.png"
  alt="Members Management 2"
  width={600}
  height={340}
  className="rounded-lg border"
/>

- Select the level of access you want to give
  - `Member`: cannot access billing information nor invite new members
  - `Admin`: can access billing information and add new members

## Change a member's access level

You can only change the access level if you are an admin of the workspace.

<img
  src="/images/help-center/datahawk-app/members-management/members-management-3.png"
  alt="Members Management 3"
  width={600}
  height={340}
  className="rounded-lg border"
/>

- Find the user you wish to change his access level
- Select the current access level (Admin or Member)
- You will see options (Admin or Member)
- Select the access level you want to give

## Remove a member from a workspace

- Find the user you wish to change his access level
- Select the current access level (Admin or Member)
- You will see options
- Select `Remove From Workspace`
