---
title: Workspace Management
description: Organize teams and data efficiently with separate workspaces for different business units or projects.
---

A <Term>workspace</Term> is an isolated environment for a brand, client, or business unit. Each one has its own data, settings, and members. Use **Settings → Workspace** to create new workspaces, monitor your current usage against your plan, and request upgrades.

## Create a new workspace

There are two ways to create a new workspace. Use whichever entry point you're closer to.

<Tabs items={['From Settings', 'From the home page']}>

<Tab value="From Settings">

<Steps>

<Step>
### Open Settings → Workspace

<img
  src="/images/help-center/datahawk-app/workspace-management/workspace-management-0.png"
  alt="Settings > Workspace"
  width={300}
  height={170}
  className="rounded-lg border"
/>
</Step>

<Step>
### Click Create Workspace

In the top-right corner, click **+ Create Workspace**.
<img
  src="/images/help-center/datahawk-app/workspace-management/workspace-management-2.png"
  alt="Settings > Workspace click"
  width={1200}
  height={680}
  className="rounded-lg border"
/>
</Step>

<Step>
### Fill in workspace details

Enter the workspace name, your phone number, company size, company name, and a short description of what you do.
</Step>

<Step>
### Submit

Click **Submit** to create the workspace.
</Step>

</Steps>

</Tab>

<Tab value="From the home page">

<Steps>

<Step>
### Open the workspace switcher

Click your **workspace name** in the top-left corner.

<img
  src="/images/help-center/datahawk-app/workspace-management/workspace-management-3.gif"
  alt="Click workspace name to open switcher"
  width={1200}
  height={680}
  className="rounded-lg border"
/>
</Step>

<Step>
### Click + Add Workspace

In the dropdown, select **+ Add Workspace**.
</Step>

<Step>
### Fill in workspace details

Enter the workspace name, your phone number, company size, company name, and a short description of what you do.
</Step>

<Step>
### Submit

Click **Submit** to create the workspace.
</Step>

</Steps>

</Tab>

</Tabs>

<Callout type="info">
  New workspaces start fresh. They don't copy settings, sources, or members from existing ones.
</Callout>

## Request an upgrade

If you're maxing out your workspace capabilities and want to upgrade, click **Request to upgrade**: Our team will reach out to discuss options.

## Update workspace information

To change your workspace name or other workspace-level settings, navigate to **Settings → Account** from inside the workspace you want to edit. See [Manage your Account](/help-center/datahawk-app/account-management) for the available fields.
