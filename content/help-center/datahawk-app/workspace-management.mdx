Go on your DataHawk WebApp \> Settings \> Workspace

## Create a New Workspace

- Settings \> Workspace

{/* TODO: Replace this Notion image with a local copy in /public/images/datahawk-app/ */}

- Top right corner `+ Create Workspace`
- Enter workspace details
- Click `Submit`

It is also possible to create a New Workspace through your home page of the DataHawk WebApp

{/* TODO: Replace this Notion image with a local copy in /public/images/datahawk-app/ */}

1. Click your **workspace name** (top-left corner)
2. From the dropdown, select `+ Add Workspace`
3. Enter workspace details
   - What will be the name of your workspace?
   - What's your mobile phone number?
   - What is your company size?
   - What's the name of your company?
   - What best describes you?
4. Click `Submit`

<Callout type="info">
  New workspaces start fresh - they don't copy settings from existing ones.
</Callout>
