## Orientation

### Get access to DataHawk

- You sign your order form
- You will receive an email with a link to our [DataHawk Platform](https://analytics.datahawk.co/)
- You can proceed to sign in
- DataHawk team will add capacities to your workspace according to your agreement.

### Key Concepts & Definitions

- **Platform:** The central environment where you configure data sources, apps, and outputs.
- **Workspace:** An isolated environment for a brand, client, or segment. Each workspace has its own data, settings, and configuration.
- **Sources:** The data you connect and track (Amazon/Walmart accounts, products, keywords, categories).
- **Dashboard Templates:** Pre-built dashboards (Power BI, Looker Studio) that you can customize for reporting.
- **Apps:** DataHawk's feature modules that help you analyze and act on your data:
  - **AI Sherlock**: Diagnoses performance issues using AI
  - **AI Copywriter**: Generates optimized product listings
  - **AI Insights**: Surfaces trends, anomalies, and recommendations
  - **Alerts**: Sends notifications for important product changes
