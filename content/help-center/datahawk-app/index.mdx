The DataHawk App is the web platform where you configure data sources, run AI modules such as Sherlock and AI Copywriter, manage Alerts, and explore your data. This page covers how to get signed in for the first time and how the platform is structured. For term definitions, see the [Glossary](/help-center/glossary).

## Get access to DataHawk

- You signed your order form
- You will receive an email with a link to our [DataHawk Platform](https://analytics.datahawk.co/)
- You can proceed to sign in
- DataHawk team will add capacities to your <Term>workspace</Term> according to your agreement.
