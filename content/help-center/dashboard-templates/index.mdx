DataHawk provides ready-to-use dashboard templates on top of your connected warehouse. Use this section to choose the right template for your reporting workflow, then follow the setup guide for the BI tool you use.

## Power BI templates

Power BI templates are built for deep analysis on top of your <Term>Snowflake</Term> database. They are best when you need detailed filtering, operational drilldowns, and reusable reporting across teams.
