Keyword tracking captures where your products appear in Amazon's search results — the foundation of both SEO and PPC analysis. For each tracked keyword, DataHawk scans the first 5 pages of search results daily, giving you historical visibility into organic rank movements, content recommendations, and (with Premium Keywords) sponsored rank as well.

## DataHawk Product Tracking Capabilities

### What data does DataHawk collect from Amazon?

For each keyword you track, DataHawk will provide a daily snapshot of these primary product attributes:

- organic search rank
- organic page rank
- sponsored page rank *(requires Amazon Premium Keywords)*
- content recommendations

Each product will contain basic information such as:

- title
- price
- stars rating
- numbers of ratings, including reviews

### What data does DataHawk collect from Walmart?

For each keyword you track, DataHawk will provide a daily snapshot of these primary product attributes:

- organic search rank
- organic page rank
- sponsored page rank
- editorial recommendations

Each product will contain basic information such as:

- title
- price
- stars rating
- numbers of ratings, including reviews
- A+ status
- product image

### Supported regions

DataHawk integration works for tracked products in all Amazon regions:

- **North America**: US, Canada, Mexico, and Brazil.
- **Europe**: UK, Germany, Italy, France, Spain, Netherlands, UAE, Sweden, India, and Poland.
- **Far East**: Japan, Singapore, and Australia.

<Callout type="info">
  Keyword tracking is country-specific. If you track the same keyword in multiple marketplaces, each <Term>marketplace</Term> is counted as a separate tracked keyword.
</Callout>

### Automated Data Sync & Backup

Once you add a product for tracking, we collect data automatically from that point forward (it may take up to 24-hours to have complete data). No historical data possible.

We then **securely store and back up** all your data **every day**, enabling **more comprehensive reporting with extended historical insights**.

### Refresh Frequency

Data is updated daily. There may be a delay in updating the data from the last 24-hours.

## How to track an Amazon Keyword

<Steps>

<Step>

### Go to the Sources Page

Go to the [Sources Page](https://analytics.datahawk.co/app/sources).

</Step>

<Step>

### Add Source

Click **Add Source**.

</Step>

<Step>

### Select Amazon or Walmart Keyword

Select **Amazon** or **Walmart**, then choose **Keyword**.

  src="/images/help-center/data-setup/keywords-tracking/keywords-tracking-1.png"
  alt="Keywords Tracking 1"
  width={600}

  className="rounded-lg border"
/>

</Step>

<Step>

### Choose your preferred method

Choose your preferred method:

- **Manual Entry**
- **CSV Import** (recommended for more than 500 keywords)

<Accordions>
  <Accordion title="Adding via Manual Entry">
    1. Select `Enter Keywords`
    2. Select Marketplace
    3. Enter or paste the keywords.
    4. (Optional) [Assign tags](/help/data-setup/tags) for better organization.
    5. Click **`Add Keywords`** to finish.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Adding via CSV Import">
    1. Download the CSV template.
    2. Fill in the template with:

    <img
  src="/images/help-center/data-setup/keywords-tracking/keywords-tracking-2.png"
  alt="Keywords Tracking 2"
  width={1200}
  height={680}
  className="rounded-lg border"
/>

       - **Marketplace:** Domain name.
       - **Keyword:** The search term you want to track.
       - [**Tags (Optional):**](/help/data-setup/tags) Use semicolons (`;`) to separate multiple tags.

    3. Save the file in **CSV UTF-8 format**.
    4. Upload the CSV file.
    5. Review and correct any errors if needed.
    6. Click **Finish**.
  </Accordion>
</Accordions>

</Step>

</Steps>
