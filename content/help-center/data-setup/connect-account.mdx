Walmart Connect is Walmart's advertising platform — the equivalent of Amazon Advertising for Walmart Marketplace sellers. Once connected, DataHawk pulls campaign-level performance daily and joins it with your Marketplace sales and search data for a complete view of advertising ROI.

## What you get

### Supported regions

DataHawk connects only with **US-registered** Walmart Connect accounts.

### Data coverage

Walmart Connect data lands in the `ADVERTISING` schema, with all tables prefixed `WMT_` (for example `ADVERTISING.WMT_SP_AD_ITEM`). The full reference — every table, what's in it, and how to use it — lives on [Walmart Advertising Data](/help-center/knowledge-hub/wmt-advertising-data).

### Historical data

At connection time, DataHawk retrieves up to **90 days** of past data. From then on we refresh daily and keep the full history available for trend analyses. Walmart finalizes reports on a 14-day lag, so a second reconciliation pass runs 15 days after the first download to capture the final numbers.

## Before you start

You'll need:

- Your **advertising ID** — see [Walmart's official guide](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/s/guides?language=en_US&article=000009632) for where to find it.
- To know whether your account is **1P or 3P** — see [Walmart user roles and permissions](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/s/guides?language=en_US&article=000009334) if you're unsure.

## How to connect

<Step>
### Go to the Sources page

Open the [Sources page](https://analytics.datahawk.co/app/sources) in the DataHawk web app.
</Step>

<Step>
### Add a source

Click **Sources** in the sidebar, then **+ Add Sources** in the top-right corner.

<Step>
### Select Walmart Marketplace Account

</Step>

<Step>
### Enter your Advertising ID

Paste the advertising ID you located earlier.

<img
  src="/images/help-center/data-setup/connect-account/connect-account-2.png"

<Step>
### Select your seller type

Pick **1P** or **3P** to match your Walmart account.

<Step>
### Confirm the connection

  src="/images/help-center/data-setup/connect-account/connect-account-4.png"
