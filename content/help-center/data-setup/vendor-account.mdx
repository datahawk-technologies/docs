Amazon Vendor Central is an invitation-only platform where vendors sell products wholesale to Amazon (the 1P model). Amazon then resells those products to consumers at prices Amazon controls. Connect your account to DataHawk to **automatically collect sales, product margins, inventory, and traffic data** — with no limit on the number of Vendor Central accounts you can link.

## DataHawk Connection Capabilities

### Supported regions

DataHawk integration works for Vendors registered in all Amazon regions:

- **North America**: US, Canada, Mexico, and Brazil.
- **Europe**: UK, Germany, Italy, France, Spain, Netherlands, UAE, Sweden, India, and Poland.
- **Far East**: Japan, Singapore, and Australia.

### Data Coverage

The list below highlights a very high-level summary of the currently available datasets on any destination.

All of the data listed below is available at **both Account-level and <Term>ASIN</Term>-level**.

- **Vendor's Revenue** (Referred to as "Shipped <Term>COGS</Term>," as this is <Term>COGS</Term> from Amazon's perspective)
- **Amazon's Revenue** (Referred to as "Shipped Sales" or "Ordered Sales," as these are sales from Amazon's perspective)
- **Units Sold**
- **Returns**
- **Product Margins**
- **Product Page Views**
- **Inventory**
- **Demand Forecasts**

### Historical data storage

Once you connect your Vendor Central account to DataHawk, we immediately retrieve **3 years of historical data**. We then **securely store and back up** all your data **everyday**, enabling **more comprehensive reporting with extended historical context**.

### Refresh Frequency & Data Delay

Data is updated daily.

For Vendor Central data, there is a 72-hour delay between the data available and the day the data is retrieved/refreshed, due to limitations with Amazon's API.

## DataHawk Limitations

- Data for the last 48h may be incomplete
- Due to Amazon's API's limitations, datasets contain data until 72 hours before the refresh.
- Some metrics are limited by Amazon API
- Initial historical depth is limited by Amazon API
- Due to Amazon's API limitations, we have limited access to data when your marketplace is sourcing and not manufacturing. [Learn more.](/help-center/data-metrics-guides/foundation/key-concepts#amazon-vendor-views-sourcing-vs-manufacturing)

## Outputs

### Raw data

Raw data can be accessed via destinations and can notably be used to build dashboards on your favorite BI solution.

When you set up a destination, DataHawk provides you with a hosted database containing your Sources data. These tables contain a very exhaustive list of data points as columns and are organized into what are called schemas.

All data is available under the `SELLING_PARTNER` schema.

Most of these tables have several variations available based on the **Distributor View** chosen. Moreover, most of these tables have a column indicating the **Selling Program** corresponding to each data point. The following are definitions of the different distributor views and selling programs available:

- **Distributor Views**
  - **Manufacturing** - Includes all product's under the Vendor's brand, including those not sourced directly from their inventory.
  - **Sourcing** - Includes only products sourced directly from the Vendor's inventory.
- **Selling Program**
  - **Retail** - The default selling program applicable to the vast majority of Vendor sales.
  - **Fresh** - Refers to [Amazon Fresh](https://www.amazon.com/fmc/m/30003175?almBrandId=QW1hem9uIEZyZXNo), Amazon's online food shopping home delivery service.
  - **Business** - Refers to [Amazon Business](https://business.amazon.com/en/discover-more/blog/what-is-amazon-business), which is specialized towards enterprises as buyers.

To see an exhaustive view of all columns within these tables, refer to [our Table & Column Referential](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1gYGqKQP9Z71nz1lOb4y0wJ15i27NvsUzdliIJeIx0HY/edit#gid=175439716).

| Schema | Table | About |
|---|---|---|
| SELLING_PARTNER | `VENDOR_SALES_{VIEW}_SRC` | Sales data including Vendor's Revenue (Shipped COGS), Amazon's Revenue (Sales), Units Sold, and Returns. |
| SELLING_PARTNER | `VENDOR_NET_PURE_PRODUCT_MARGIN_SRC` | Net Pure Margins per Product |
| SELLING_PARTNER | `VENDOR_TRAFFIC_SRC` | Daily Page Views per Product |
| SELLING_PARTNER | `VENDOR_INVENTORY_{VIEW}_SRC` | In-Depth Daily Inventory Breakdown |
| SELLING_PARTNER | `VENDOR_FORECASTING_SRC` | Demand Forecasts per <Term>ASIN</Term> provided on a weekly basis. Includes Mean, P70, P80, and P90 forecasts. A P70 forecast means that if the Vendor stocks up on the recommended about, there is a 70% chance of avoiding a shortage. |
