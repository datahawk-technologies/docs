## Your Seller Central Account

Amazon Seller Central is the platform Sellers use to sell products on Amazon directly to consumers, either by fulfilling it themselves (<Term>FBM</Term>) or via Amazon's fulfillment centers (<Term>FBA</Term>).

DataHawk allows you to **add an unlimited number of Seller Central accounts** you manage.

By linking your Amazon Seller Central account, DataHawk automatically collects and organizes the following data for **seamless reporting and analysis**:

- **Financial Data:** Revenue, expenses, and cost breakdowns **by account and product**
- **Traffic & Sessions Data:** Page views and sessions metrics **per product**
- **Inventory Data:** <Term>FBA</Term> inventory levels and movements **by product**

## DataHawk Connexion Capabilities

### Supported regions

DataHawk integration works for Sellers registered in all Amazon regions:

- **North America**: US, Canada, Mexico, and Brazil.
- **Europe**: UK, Germany, Italy, France, Spain, Netherlands, UAE, Sweden, India, and Poland.
- **Far East**: Japan, Singapore, and Australia.

### Historical data storage

Once you connect your Seller Central account to DataHawk, we immediately retrieve a maximum of **2 years of historical data on some data point**. We then **securely store and back up** all your data **everyday**, enabling **more comprehensive reporting with extended historical insights**.

### Refresh Frequency & Data Delay

Data is updated daily.

There is usually a delay in updating the data from the last 24 hours.

## DataHawk Limitations

- Data for the last 48h may be incomplete
- Due to Amazon's API's limitations, datasets contain data until 72 hours before the refresh.
- Some metrics are limited by Amazon API
- Initial historical depth is limited by Amazon API

## Outputs

### Raw data

Raw data can be accessed on your destination and can notably be **used to build your own dashboards** on your favorite BI solution.

Your Seller Central accounts data will be found in the following schemas:

| Schema | Available Data |
|---|---|
| FINANCE | Sales Data and Profit & Loss Data |
| RAW_INVENTORY | Inventory Breakdown Data |
| SELLING_PARTNER | Traffic & Sessions Data |

Detailed documentation on these data schemas can be found in the [Datasets Models](/help-center/knowledge-hub/datasets-models) page.

### Dashboards

**Ready-to-use dashboards** and reports are available on the web application, and in our [pre-built Power BI report](https://drive.google.com/uc?export=download&id=1AIvk0lBOnNdMqHrXopZLrNW-pFWGTVtR) covering Seller Finances & Supply Chain (Traffic Data coming soon).
