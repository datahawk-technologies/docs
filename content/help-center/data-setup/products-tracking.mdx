Product tracking monitors key data from Amazon and Walmart product pages, ratings, sales rank, sales, <Term>buy box</Term> ownership, and dozens of other attributes, so you can track performance, detect changes, and make informed decisions. Each tracked product produces a daily snapshot that feeds dashboards, Alerts, and Sherlock downstream.

<Callout type="info">
  By combining it with [Keywords Tracking](/help-center/data-setup/keywords-tracking), you can monitor the changes in the rankings of your tracked products.
</Callout>

## DataHawk Product Tracking Capabilities

### What data does DataHawk collect?

For each tracked product, DataHawk captures a daily snapshot of dozens of attributes. The fields vary slightly between marketplaces. Pick your platform.

<Tabs items={['Amazon', 'Walmart']}>

<Tab value="Amazon">

- brand
- title
- description
- bullet items
- product image
- number of media
- the product's price
- stars rating
- number of ratings
- the number of Sellers competing for the <Term>Buy Box</Term>
- displayed categories and corresponding best seller ranks
- its group <Term>ASIN</Term>
- number of variations
- A+ status
- out-of-stock status
- and many more.

Plus these computed metrics:

- Estimated Monthly Sales
- Competitiveness Score
- Listing Quality Score

</Tab>

<Tab value="Walmart">

- brand
- title
- description
- bullet items
- product image
- number of media
- the product's price
- stars rating
- number of ratings, including reviews
- the number of Sellers competing for the <Term>Buy Box</Term>
- displayed categories and corresponding best seller ranks
- its group Item ID
- number of variations
- Walmart+ status
- activated product-page features, including About the Brand when the marketing module is active
- out-of-stock status
- and many more.

</Tab>

</Tabs>

### Supported regions

DataHawk integration works for tracked products in all Amazon regions:

- **North America**: US, Canada, Mexico, and Brazil.
- **Europe**: UK, Germany, Italy, France, Spain, Netherlands, UAE, Sweden, India, and Poland.
- **Far East**: Japan, Singapore, and Australia.

<Callout type="info">
  Product tracking is country-specific. If you track the same product in multiple marketplaces, each <Term>marketplace</Term> is counted as a separate tracked product.
</Callout>

### Automated Data Sync & Backup

Once you add a product for tracking, we collect data automatically from that point forward (it may take up to 24-hours to have complete data). No historical data possible.

We then **securely store and back up** all your data **every day**, enabling **more comprehensive reporting with extended historical context**.

### Refresh Frequency

Data is updated daily. There may be a delay in updating the data from the last 24-hours.

<Tab value="CSV Import">

**Recommended for more than 500 products.** Lets you set tags and variation rules per row in a spreadsheet.

1. Download the CSV template.
2. Fill in the template with:
   - **Marketplace**: Domain name (Amazon/Walmart marketplace).
   - **Product ID**: <Term>ASIN</Term> (Amazon) or Product ID (Walmart).
   - **Include Variations**: `True` to include variations, `False` to exclude.
   - **Tags (optional)**: Separate multiple tags with semicolons (`;`).
3. Save the file in **CSV UTF-8 format**.
4. Upload the CSV file.
5. Review and correct any errors if needed.
6. Click **Finish**.

</Tab>

## How to archive a tracked product

Archiving stops collecting new data for a product but keeps its history intact. Use it when you no longer need fresh snapshots for a SKU you used to track.

<Steps>

<Step>
### Open your tracked products list

In the DataHawk App, go to **Sources → Products**.
</Step>

<Step>
### Find the products you want to archive

Use the search to locate a specific product, or scroll the list to pick several.
</Step>

<Step>
### Select the products

Tick the box next to each product you want to archive.

<img
  src="/images/help-center/data-setup/products-tracking/products-tracking-8.png"
  alt="Tick the checkbox next to each product"
  width={1200}
  height={680}
  className="rounded-lg border"
/>

To archive everything you currently track, click **Select all products** instead.

<img
  src="/images/help-center/data-setup/products-tracking/products-tracking-9.png"
  alt="Click Select all products to archive every tracked product at once"
  width={1200}
  height={680}
  className="rounded-lg border"
/>
</Step>

<Step>
### Archive

Click **Archive in <Term>Workspace</Term>**. The selected products stop receiving new daily snapshots immediately.

<img
  src="/images/help-center/data-setup/products-tracking/products-tracking-10.png"
  alt="Click Archive in Workspace to finish"
  width={1200}
  height={680}
  className="rounded-lg border"
/>
</Step>

</Steps>
