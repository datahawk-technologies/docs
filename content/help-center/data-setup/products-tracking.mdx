## Product tracking

Product tracking at DataHawk **monitors key data from Amazon** product pages (e.g., ratings, sales rank, sales, and buy box ownership) so businesses can **track performance, detect changes, and make informed decisions**.

<Callout type="info">
  By combining it with [Amazon Keywords Tracking](/help/data-setup/keywords-tracking), you can monitor the changes in the rankings of your tracked products.
</Callout>

## DataHawk Product Tracking Capabilities

### What data does DataHawk collect from Amazon?

For each product you track on Amazon, DataHawk will provide a daily snapshot of the main attributes, such as:

- brand
- title
- description
- bullet items
- product image
- number of media
- the product's price
- stars rating
- numbers of ratings
- the number of Sellers competing for the Buy Box
- displayed categories and corresponding best seller ranks
- its group ASIN
- number of variations
- A+ status
- out-of-stock status
- and many more.

We further include these computed metrics:

- its Estimated Monthly Sales
- a Competitiveness Score
- and a Listing Quality Score

### What data does DataHawk collect from Walmart?

For each product you track, DataHawk will provide a daily snapshot of the main attributes such as:

- brand
- title
- description
- bullet items
- product image
- number of media
- the product's price
- stars rating
- numbers of ratings, including reviews
- the number of Sellers competing for the Buy Box
- displayed categories and corresponding best seller ranks
- its group ASIN
- number of variations
- Walmart+ status
- out-of-stock status
- and many more…

### Supported regions

DataHawk integration works for tracked products in all Amazon regions:

- **North America**: US, Canada, Mexico, and Brazil.
- **Europe**: UK, Germany, Italy, France, Spain, Netherlands, UAE, Sweden, India, and Poland.
- **Far East**: Japan, Singapore, and Australia.

<Callout type="info">
  Product tracking is country-specific. If you track the same product in multiple marketplaces, each marketplace is counted as a separate tracked product.
</Callout>

### Automated Data Sync & Backup

Once you add a product for tracking, we collect data automatically from that point forward (it may take up to 24-hours to have complete data). No historical data possible.

We then **securely store and back up** all your data **every day**, enabling **more comprehensive reporting with extended historical insights**.

### Refresh Frequency

Data is updated daily. There may be a delay in updating the data from the last 24-hours.
