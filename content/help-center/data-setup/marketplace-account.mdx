Walmart Marketplace is Walmart's third-party seller platform — the equivalent of Amazon Seller Central for the Walmart ecosystem. Connect your account to DataHawk to automatically pull sales, fulfillment, and item-level data. There's no limit on the number of US-registered Marketplace accounts you can link.

## DataHawk Connection Capabilities

### Supported regions

DataHawk connects only with US-registered accounts.

### Data Coverage

Walmart Seller data can be found in the `FINANCE` schema. Tables are prefixed with `WMT`. Example: `FINANCE.WMT_MARKETPLACE_ITEMS`

Data is refreshed **daily**.

#### Tables

- `WMT_MARKETPLACE_ITEMS`
  - This table stores product listings for Walmart Marketplace, including identifiers, pricing, availability, condition, status, and publishing details for inventory management.
- `WMT_MARKETPLACE_ORDERS`
  - This table stores Walmart Marketplace order details, including order IDs, product info, pricing, customer data, fulfillment, shipping, and status for tracking and processing.

### Historical data storage

Once you connect your Walmart Marketplace to DataHawk, we immediately retrieve a maximum of **90 days of data**. Then, we **securely store and back up** all your data **everyday**, enabling **more comprehensive reporting with extended historical context**.

### Refresh Frequency & Data Delay

- **Data Refresh**: A second data refresh takes place 15 days after the first download.
- **Data Updates**: Walmart does not update reports after 14 days, so we always fetch the most recent consolidated version available.
