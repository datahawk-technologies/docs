---
title: Setup Overview
description: Connect your accounts and configure product, keyword, and category tracking so DataHawk can collect and deliver clean data across your business.
---

Data setup is the one-time work that decides what DataHawk can show you. Connect your marketplace and advertising accounts, upload <Term>COGS</Term>, and choose which products, keywords, and categories to track — everything else (dashboards, alerts, Insights, Sherlock) reads from what you configure here. Start with whichever marketplace your business runs on.
