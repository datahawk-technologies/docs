## Category tracking

Track an Amazon category to monitor the top 100 products daily and follow their sales rank with a simplified set of product metrics.

<Callout type="info">
  Only available for Amazon
</Callout>

## DataHawk Product Tracking Capabilities

### What data does DataHawk collect?

For each category you track, DataHawk will provide you with a daily snapshot of these primary product attributes:

- category name
- category path
- top 100 products
- bestseller rank
- product price
- title
- star rating
- numbers of ratings, including reviews
- A+ status
- product image URL
- estimated Sales

### Supported regions

DataHawk integration works for tracked products in all Amazon regions:

- **North America**: US, Canada, Mexico, and Brazil.
- **Europe**: UK, Germany, Italy, France, Spain, Netherlands, UAE, Sweden, India, and Poland.
- **Far East**: Japan, Singapore, and Australia.

<Callout type="info">
  Category tracking is country-specific. If you track the same category in multiple marketplaces, each marketplace is counted as a separate tracked category.
</Callout>

### Automated Data Sync & Backup

Once you add a product for tracking, we collect data automatically from that point forward (it may take up to 24-hours to have complete data). No historical data possible.

We then **securely store and back up** all your data **every day**, enabling **more comprehensive reporting with extended historical insights**.

### Refresh Frequency

Data is updated daily. There may be a delay in updating the data from the last 24-hours.

### Adding Categories

<Steps>

<Step>

### Go to the Sources Page

Go to the [Sources Page](https://analytics.datahawk.co/app/sources).

</Step>

<Step>

### Add Source

Click **Add Source**.

</Step>

<Step>

### Select Amazon Categories

Select **Amazon**, then choose **Categories**.

</Step>

<Step>

### Search for a category

Search for the top-level category you want to add.

</Step>

<Step>

### Choose tracking scope

Choose:

- A single category.
- All subcategories below the selected level.
- A specific number of subcategory levels.

</Step>

<Step>

### Optionally remove subcategories

(Optional) Remove specific subcategories if needed.

</Step>

<Step>

### Confirm

Click **Continue** to confirm and finish adding.

</Step>

</Steps>
