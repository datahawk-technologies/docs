## How it works

When you add a destination, DataHawk creates a dedicated database hosted on either **<Term>Snowflake</Term>** or **<Term>BigQuery</Term>** (depending on the tool you connect). This database is populated with all your DataHawk data and refreshed daily — no manual exports needed.

DataHawk refreshes your database **every day**, for both <Term>Snowflake</Term> and <Term>BigQuery</Term>. However, not all data is available at the same time — some datasets are populated later in the day because Amazon's API does not deliver all data types on the same schedule. See the individual dataset pages in the Knowledge Hub for dataset-specific freshness details.

- **Snowflake** is the underlying database for Power BI, Excel, and SQL-based tools.
- **BigQuery** is the underlying database for Looker Studio and Google Sheets.

Each destination guide includes the database setup step inline — you don't need to set up the database separately before starting.

## What data is available?

All data you collect through your DataHawk <Term>workspace</Term> is available via the Destinations of your choice:

- **Product data** — tracked products, listings, sales ranks, search rankings (organic and sponsored)
- **Seller Central data** — orders, profit & loss, financial events, traffic
- **Vendor Central data** — revenue, financial events, traffic
- **Advertising data** — aggregated per account, campaign, or product
- **DataHawk KPIs** — sales estimates, keyword search volume, and more

## How to add a destination

<Steps>

<Step>

### Open Destinations

Open the DataHawk app and navigate to **Destinations** in the sidebar.

</Step>

<Step>

### Add Destination

Click **Add Destination**.

</Step>

<Step>

### Select your tool

Select the tool you want to connect (e.g. Google Sheets, Power BI). DataHawk will recommend the right hosted database for that tool — BigQuery or Snowflake.

</Step>

<Step>

### Confirm and provision

Confirm by clicking **Use BigQuery** or **Use Snowflake**. DataHawk will begin provisioning your database.

</Step>

<Step>

### Retrieve credentials

Once the status shows **Enabled**, click on the destination to retrieve your database credentials.

</Step>

<Step>

### Connect your tool

Follow the relevant guide above to connect your tool.

</Step>

</Steps>

<Callout type="info">
  Once added BigQuery or Snowflake database to your DataHawk App, we will take up to 4 hours to get everything ready.
</Callout>
