**Source:** Amazon All Listings Report (`GET_MERCHANT_LISTINGS_ALL_DATA`).

In DataHawk, this is modeled as `SELLER_INVENTORY_ALL_LISTINGS` — the latest snapshot per ASIN × SKU. For a fully historized version tracking every change over time, see `SELLER_INVENTORY_ALL_LISTINGS_HISTORY`.

## What this dataset is

A daily snapshot of your entire Amazon catalog - every SKU you've listed, regardless of fulfillment method or status. It shows how each listing is currently set up: its price, quantity, condition, and whether Amazon considers it active and buyable.

For **FBM sellers**, this is the primary place to check your live inventory levels, since FBM stock is seller-managed and doesn't appear in FBA inventory tables. For **FBA sellers**, it's most useful as a catalog health check - confirming which listings are active, suppressed, or inactive - rather than for stock quantities (FBA quantity is not populated here; use `SELLER_INVENTORY_FBA_PLANNING` for that).

## What's in it

- **Listing identity** — ASIN, SKU, FNSKU, product name, and condition. Covers your full catalog including listings that are inactive or suppressed.

- **Status** — Whether the listing is active, inactive, incomplete, or suppressed. This is the key field for diagnosing why a product isn't showing up on Amazon.

- **Pricing** — Your current selling price.

- **FBM quantity** — The quantity you have declared as available for merchant-fulfilled orders. This is the live sellable stock figure for FBM.

- **Listing details** — Open date, product group, merchant shipping group, and whether the listing is in the Buy Box.

---

## Questions you can answer

- Which of my listings are currently active vs. inactive or suppressed?
- What is my current FBM stock level by SKU and marketplace?
- What price am I listing each SKU at across marketplaces?
- Which SKUs exist in my catalog but aren't buyable right now?
- When was each listing first created?
- Do I have duplicate SKUs mapped to the same ASIN?

---

## Limitations

- **FBA quantity is not available here.** The quantity field reflects FBM stock only. For FBA inventory use `SELLER_INVENTORY_FBA_PLANNING`.
- **Snapshot only, no history.** This table always reflects the current state. To track how listings or prices changed over time, use `SELLER_INVENTORY_ALL_LISTINGS_HISTORY`.
- **No sales or velocity data.** This is a catalog snapshot, not a performance report. It won't tell you how fast SKUs are selling.
- **Suppression reasons are not included.** If a listing is suppressed, you'll see the status but not the reason. Check Seller Central directly to resolve suppression issues.
