## What to include when contacting support

The faster we can identify your account and reproduce the issue, the faster we can help. When emailing `support@datahawk.co`, include:

- Your <Term>WSID</Term>: Find it in the DataHawk app under **Setup → Workspace**
- The exact area of the product where the issue occurs (module, page, dashboard tab)
- A short description of what you did, what you expected, and what actually happened
- A screenshot or screen recording if the issue is visual
- For data discrepancies: The date range, <Term>ASIN</Term> or SKU, and a link to the source report you're comparing against

## Share your feedback

Your feedback shapes the product roadmap. If you have suggestions, feature requests, or want to brainstorm a use case with us, email `cs@datahawk.co` or send a thumbs-down on any docs page using the feedback widget at the bottom; comments come straight to the team.
