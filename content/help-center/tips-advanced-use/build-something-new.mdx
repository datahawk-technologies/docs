## Customize your own dashboard

### Adapt an existing template

You can adapt any existing template by downloading it and implementing your desired modifications, whether it's adapting the visuals, changing the metrics displayed, or even adding your own data sources.

### Build from scratch, on any destination

If you have a clear vision of what you want your eCommerce reporting to include, or if you want to build on a BI tool for which we do not offer any template yet, you can build from scratch on the tool of your choice.
