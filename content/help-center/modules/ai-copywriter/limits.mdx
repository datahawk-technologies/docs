## Marketplaces supported

All marketplaces supported by Amazon are supported by DataHawk.

## Rate limits

AI Copywriter generates each suggestion in under a minute. During peak usage:

- You may see a "<Term>capacity</Term> reached" message — wait and retry
- Suggestions may take longer than usual

## Quality variability

AI output isn't deterministic. Different refreshes of the same listing produce different suggestions. This is expected — refresh until you find a version that reads well.

## When something goes wrong

- **"Suboptimal suggestion"** — click **Refresh** to regenerate
- **"Capacity limit reached"** — wait a few minutes and try again
- **"Cannot generate"** — check that the product is fully synced in DataHawk and has up-to-date data

For persistent issues, email [support@datahawk.co](mailto:support@datahawk.co).
