## "Can DataHawk publish changes directly to Amazon?"

Not yet. Today, you copy the optimized content from DataHawk and paste it into Seller Central or Vendor Central. Direct publishing is on the roadmap.

## "Why did the AI suggest a phrase I don't like?"

AI output varies between runs. Click **Refresh** to generate a different version. If a specific phrase keeps appearing and you'd like a different angle, edit the suggestion in DataHawk before copying.

## "Does AI Copywriter work for Walmart?"

No, it doesn't at the moment.

## "What languages are supported?"

All languages for which the marketplaces are covered by Amazon.

## "How does AI Copywriter pick the keywords?"

It uses DataHawk's Keyword Research data. Keywords are ranked by:

- Monthly search volume on the relevant <Term>marketplace</Term>
- Relevance to your product
- Competition (so you don't optimize for terms you can't rank for)

## "Will my listing changes affect existing reviews?"

No. Reviews are tied to your <Term>ASIN</Term>, not your listing copy.

## "How long until Amazon shows the new content?"

Typically 24–48 hours after you save in Seller Central.

## "Why is my optimisation result poor?"

A few common causes:

- **Product info isn't fully synced** — check that all attributes are populated in your tracked product
- **Keywords are sparse for your category** — niche products with low search volume get less rich suggestions
- **The original listing is already strong** — sometimes there's not much to improve

## Roadmap

Planned enhancements:

- **Batch optimisation** for multiple listings at once
- **Optimisation Potential Score** — automatically identify which products in your catalog are best candidates for optimisation
