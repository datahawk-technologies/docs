---
title: Create your first alert
description: Walkthrough to create your first DataHawk alert — from picking a product to receiving the email.
---

<Callout type="info">
  It takes about 5 minutes.
</Callout>

## Before you start

Make sure you've already:

- Added at least one [product to track](/help-center/data-setup/products-tracking) in your <Term>workspace</Term>

## Create your alert

<Steps>

<Step>
### Open the Alerts module

From the left navigation, go to **Apps → Alerts**. If this is your first time, you'll see an empty Alerts list and a **Create alert** button at the top right.

<img
  src="/images/help-center/modules/alerts/create/create-1.png"
  alt="Create 1"
  width={1200}
  height={680}
  className="rounded-lg border"
/>
</Step>

<Step>
### Pick what you want to be alerted on

Click **Create alert**. You'll be offered two paths:

**Preset triggers** — DataHawk's most-used templates. Good if you want to get going fast. Examples:

- Going out of stock
- Losing the <Term>Buy Box</Term>
- Significant price decrease (10%+)
- Becoming a best seller
- Changes in star ratings

<img
  src="/images/help-center/modules/alerts/create/create-2.png"
  alt="Create 2"
  width={1200}
  height={680}
  className="rounded-lg border"
/>

**Custom triggers** — for when you need a specific rule. You'll pick a property, an operator, and a value. See [Custom triggers](/help-center/modules/alerts/custom-triggers) for details.

<img
  src="/images/help-center/modules/alerts/create/create-3.png"
  alt="Create 3"
  width={1200}
  height={680}
  className="rounded-lg border"
/>

For your first alert, start with a preset. We recommend **Losing the <Term>Buy Box</Term>** — it's the one most customers find immediate value in.
</Step>

<Step>
### Choose the product (or tag)

Select which product or group of products this alert applies to. Options:

- **A specific product** — paste an <Term>ASIN</Term> or pick from your tracked list

<img
  src="/images/help-center/modules/alerts/create/create-4.png"
  alt="Create 4"
  width={1200}
  height={680}
  className="rounded-lg border"
/>

- **All products with a tag** — e.g. "Top sellers" or "Hero ASINs"

<img
  src="/images/help-center/modules/alerts/create/create-5.png"
  alt="Create 5"
  width={1200}
  height={680}
  className="rounded-lg border"
/>

Tags are useful when you want one rule to cover many products. See [Using Tags](/help-center/data-setup/tags).
</Step>

<Step>
### Set up the recipient group

Recipient groups decide who gets the email. You can:

- **Use yourself** — your DataHawk login email, by default
- **Create a group** — add team members and reuse it for other alerts

<img
  src="/images/help-center/modules/alerts/create/create-6.png"
  alt="Create 6"
  width={1200}
  height={680}
  className="rounded-lg border"
/>

Create one called **"My team"** if you'll share alerts with colleagues — it saves time later.
</Step>

<Step>
### Name and save your alert

Give a name to your alert, then click **Create my Alert**. Your alert is now live.

<img
  src="/images/help-center/modules/alerts/create/create-7.png"
  alt="Create 7"
  width={1200}
  height={680}
  className="rounded-lg border"
/>
</Step>

</Steps>

DataHawk checks the conditions daily and sends an email when something matches. From the Alerts list, you can also:

- Pause an alert without deleting it
- Edit the trigger, products, or recipients
- Delete an alert you no longer need

<img
  src="/images/help-center/modules/alerts/create/create-8.png"
  alt="Create 8"
  width={1200}
  height={680}
  className="rounded-lg border"
/>
