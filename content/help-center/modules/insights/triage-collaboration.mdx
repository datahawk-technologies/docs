Insights are most useful when they're triaged — an unassigned, unsorted feed grows noisy fast. This page walks through the workflow the Insights module is built around: filter down to what matters, assign each one to a teammate, then set a status that signals what happened next.

## Filtering

Use the filter selector at the top of your Insights list to narrow what you see.

**By source:**

- Ads account
- Seller Central account
- Product
- <Term>Marketplace</Term>

**By insight property:**

- **Assignee** — show insights assigned to you, a teammate, or unassigned
- **Practice** — Ads, SEO, Finance, Market, Product
- **Event Date** — when the underlying anomaly or finding occurred
- **Send Date** — when the insight was generated and sent
- **Hide Closed** — hides insights with status Done, Expected, or No Action Needed

## Assigning insights

When a new insight comes in, you can assign it to yourself or any user in your <Term>workspace</Term>. Assignment helps you:

- Make ownership clear
- Track who's working on what
- Keep the list tidy

Anyone in the workspace can reassign an insight.

## Status workflow

Every insight starts at **New**. Update it as you work:

| Status | Use when... |
|---|---|
| **New** | Insight just landed and hasn't been touched |
| **In Progress** | Someone's actively working on it |
| **Done** | The recommendation has been implemented |
| **Expected** | The insight reflects something you already knew or planned (e.g. promotional spike) |
| **No Action Needed** | You've reviewed and decided no change is required |

Closed statuses (Done, Expected, No Action Needed) can be hidden from the list using the **Hide Closed** filter.

## Giving feedback

Your feedback helps us improve future insights.

**General feedback** — click **Submit feedback** at the top of the Insights module to send notes or suggest new insight types.

**Per-insight feedback** — every insight has thumbs-up and thumbs-down icons. Use them to flag what's helpful and what isn't.

**Competitive Products Weekly Digest** — within each digest, you can mark whether the listed competitors are relevant. This refines future digests.

## Email digest

By default you'll get a summary email every business day. To control this:

- **Toggle off the email** at the top of the Insights list to receive nothing
