## What Insights does

Once your accounts are connected, DataHawk analyses your data daily and surfaces three types of notification:

- **Findings** — actionable recommendations based on your data (e.g. ad campaigns to review, listings to refresh)
- **Anomalies** — unexpected changes flagged automatically (e.g. sudden sales drops, unusual price moves)
- **Good Practices** — eCommerce essentials to set you up for success (not based on your data — universally applicable)

Insights covers your full business: ads, sales, finance, SEO, products, and market.

## When to use Insights

Use Insights when you want **DataHawk to bring you the issues** rather than going hunting yourself.

| You want to... | Use Insights? |
|---|---|
| Discover patterns without building a query | ✅ Yes |
| Get a daily feed of what changed | ✅ Yes |
| Be told the moment a specific thing happens | Use Alerts instead |
| Understand *why* a metric moved | Use Sherlock instead |

## What you need

Different insights need different sources. The more you connect, the more you'll receive.

| Prerequisite | What you'll get |
|---|---|
| Products tracked and tagged as **Owned** | SEO, Product, Market anomalies; "Improve listings organically"; "Competitive products weekly digest" |
| **Ads account** connected | Ad-related Findings (effective and ineffective campaigns); Ad anomalies |
| **Seller Central account** connected | Finance anomalies (gross sales, units sold, fees); some Findings |

## Where to find Insights

Open **Apps → Insights** in the left navigation. The list shows all current insights, with filters by source, practice, date, and assignee.

You'll also receive a **summary email every business day**. Toggle this off in the Insights list if you'd rather check in manually.
