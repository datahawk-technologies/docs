Most Insights questions come down to a handful of patterns — accounts not yet connected, products not tagged as Owned, default filters hiding the feed, or marketplace limits. Skim the headings for the symptom that matches yours, then jump in.

<Accordions>

<Accordion title="I'm not seeing any insights yet">
A few common reasons:

- **Accounts aren't connected** — most insights need at least one connected source. See [Insights overview](/help-center/modules/insights) for which insight needs which connection.
- **Products aren't tagged as Owned** — many insights are scoped to "products you sell." Tag them in Sources.
- **It's too soon** — insights are generated daily. Allow up to 24 hours after connecting accounts.
- **Filters are hiding them** — check the filter bar; **Hide Closed** is on by default for many users.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How long after connecting accounts before I see something?">
Up to 24 hours for daily insights. Weekly Findings (e.g. competitive digest) appear on their next weekly cycle.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What's the difference between Insights and Sherlock?">
- **Insights** flags *what* is happening — anomalies, opportunities, recommendations. Pattern detection at scale across your account.
- **<Term>Sherlock</Term>** explains *why* something happened — root-cause analysis on a specific change.

They're complementary. An anomaly in Insights ("Gross Sales dropped Monday") becomes a Sherlock investigation ("why?").
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Does Insights work with Walmart?">
No, Insights are working only for Amazon users.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I customize which insights I receive?">
Today, the simplest control is the email-digest toggle and using filters to focus the in-app view.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I share insights with my team?">
Yes — assign them to teammates and use status to track progress. See [Triaging & collaboration](/help-center/modules/insights/triage-collaboration).
</Accordion>

</Accordion>
