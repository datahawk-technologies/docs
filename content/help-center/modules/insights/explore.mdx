A short walkthrough to start receiving and acting on DataHawk Insights.

## Before you start

Make sure you've connected:

- Your **Amazon Seller** or **Vendor** account (for sales-based insights)
- Your **Amazon Advertising** account (for ad-related insights)
- At least one tracked product, ideally tagged as **Owned** so we know which products are yours

The more sources connected, the more insights you'll receive. See the [Insights overview](/help-center/modules/insights) for which insights need which connections.

## Step 1: Open Insights

From the left navigation, go to **Apps → Insights**.

If it's your first visit, the list may be empty. Insights are generated daily from your data, so allow up to 24 hours after connecting your accounts before expecting your first feed.

## Step 2: Read your first insight

Each insight has:

- **A title** — the headline finding (e.g. *"Sales rank dropped 30% week-over-week"*)
- **A practice** — the area it relates to (Ads, SEO, Finance, Market, Product)

Click an insight to see the supporting chart and details.

## Step 3: Triage it

For each insight, you can:

- **Assign** it to yourself or a teammate
- **Set a status** — In Progress, Done, Expected, or No Action Needed
- **Give feedback** — thumbs up or thumbs down, so we improve future insights

Keep your list clean by closing out insights you've reviewed.
