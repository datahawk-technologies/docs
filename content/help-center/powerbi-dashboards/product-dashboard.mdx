The Product dashboard lets you monitor performance across your tracked products on Amazon and Walmart. Use it to track rankings, pricing, ratings, listing quality, and estimated sales — for your own products and the top products in your tracked categories.

## Questions this dashboard helps answer

- Which of my products have the best and worst Best Sellers Rank (<Term>BSR</Term>) right now?
- How are my product ratings and review counts trending over time?
- What is my <Term>Buy Box</Term> win rate, and which sellers are winning it instead of me?
- How does my average listing quality score compare across my catalog?
- Which products have had price changes, and by how much?
- How are estimated monthly sales and units trending for my products?
- Which products have seen changes in their listing details (title, images, badges)?

## Before you start

**What you need:**

- [Products tracked](/help-center/data-setup/products-tracking) in DataHawk (Amazon and/or Walmart)
- Optionally: tracked categories, to also see the top 100 products within those categories

**Setup:** Make sure you have downloaded and configured the Power BI template. See [Connect to Power BI](/help-center/connect-tools/powerbi).

## Dashboard tabs at a glance

| Tab | What it shows |
|---|---|
| **Dashboard** | A high-level overview of key product metrics for the selected period |
| **Overview** | A detailed snapshot of your full product portfolio at a point in time |
| **History** | How metrics have changed over time (<Term>BSR</Term>, price, ratings, estimated sales) |
| **Changes** | A log of specific changes detected in product details |

## Tab by tab

**Dashboard** — A summary view showing the most important metrics for your tracked products across the selected date range. Use this as your daily health check — it surfaces top-level figures for BSR, pricing, <Term>Buy Box</Term>, ratings, and estimated sales in one place.

**Overview** — A detailed, product-by-product snapshot covering all key attributes: variants, brands, sellers, pricing (min/avg/max), Buy Box ownership, listing quality score (LQS), images, badges, sessions, and conversion rate. Use filters to drill down by <Term>marketplace</Term>, seller account, brand, product tag, or category.

**History** — A time-series view showing how your products' metrics have evolved. Useful for spotting trends — for example, a drop in BSR following a price change, or a spike in ratings after a product launch. You can filter by date range, product, or marketplace.

**Changes** — A changelog that flags when specific product details have changed: title, price, images, category, and other listing attributes. Use this to catch unexpected listing modifications (by you or other sellers) and to audit your own updates.

## Filters

All tabs share the same set of filters. Key filters to know:

| Filter | What it does |
|---|---|
| **Dates** | Set the date range for analysis |
| **Marketplace** | Filter to one or more Amazon/Walmart marketplaces |
| **Seller** | Filter to a specific seller account |
| **Brand** | Filter by brand name |
| **Product Name** | Filter by product name |
| **Product ID** | Filter by <Term>ASIN</Term> (Amazon) or equivalent ID (Walmart) |
| **Product Tag** | Filter to products assigned a specific tag in DataHawk |
| **Category** | Filter by product category |
| **Price** | Set a price range |
| **Ratings** | Filter by total number of ratings |
| **Rating** | Filter by average star rating |
| **Sales Rank** | Filter by BSR range |

## For analysts & developers

### Data models

The Product dashboard pulls from two data models:

- **Products** — [Amazon](/help-center/data-setup/products-tracking) / [Walmart](/help-center/data-setup/products-tracking)
- **Categories** — [Amazon](/help-center/data-setup/categories-tracking) / [Walmart](/help-center/data-setup/categories-tracking)

Product data comes from your tracked products. Category data covers the top 100 products within each of your tracked categories.

### Key metrics reference

**Product overview**

- **Variants** — Count of distinct product variants
- **Brands** — Count of unique brands in your portfolio
- **Sellers** — Count of competing sellers
- **Sellers (Winners)** — Count of distinct Buy Box winners
- **Seller (Latest)** — Most recent Buy Box winner

**Price**

- **Price (Min / Avg / Max)** — Minimum, average, and maximum prices
- **Price (Distinct)** — Count of distinct price points

**Buy Box**

- **Buy Box % (Active)** — Buy Box enabled rate (competitive positioning)
- **Buy Box % (Public)** — Buy Box share visible to customers
- **Buy Box % (Private)** — Average owned Buy Box percentage (requires Seller Central connection)

**Sales & units** *(estimates)*

- **Units/mo (E)(Avg)(Low / High)** — Estimated monthly units, low and high range
- **Sales (E)(Low / High)** — Total estimated sales, low and high range
- **Sales % (E)** — Your products' share of total estimated market sales
- **Sales Score** — Estimated demand score

**Rankings**

- **BSR (Best / Avg / Worst)** — Best Sellers Rank: minimum, average, and maximum across your portfolio
- **Categories** — Number of categories the product appears in

**Ratings & reviews**

- **Rating (Avg)** — Average star rating
- **Ratings (Min / Avg / Max)** — Distribution of total review counts

**Listing quality**

- **Images (Avg)** — Average number of listing images
- **LQS (Avg)** — Average Listing Quality Score
- **Badges** — Count of badges (e.g. Amazon's Choice, Best Seller)

**Traffic** *(requires Seller Central connection)*

- **Sessions** — Total sessions on product pages
- **Pageviews** — Total page views
- **Sessions CVR (Avg)** — Average session-to-purchase conversion rate
