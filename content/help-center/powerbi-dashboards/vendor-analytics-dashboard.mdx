DataHawk's Vendor Analytics Dashboard gives Vendor Central managers a complete view of their business with Amazon — covering traffic, sales, inventory, advertising, and share of voice analysis. It is designed for weekly and monthly reporting, with up to two years of history for trend analysis.

## Questions this dashboard helps answer

- How many glance views did my products receive, and how many converted to shipped units?
- What is my shipped revenue and shipped <Term>COGS</Term>, and how are they trending year over year?
- What is my current sellable on-hand inventory, and which products are at risk of going out of stock?
- What is my advertising spend as a percentage of my total shipped revenue (<Term>TACoS</Term>)?
- How is my share of voice evolving for the keywords I'm tracking?
- Which of my purchase orders have low confirmation or fill rates?
- Which brands, products, or markets are driving the biggest changes in my metrics this period?

## Before you start

**What you need:**

- Vendor Central account connected in DataHawk
- Advertising account connected in DataHawk (required for advertising tabs)

**Set up:** Download and configure the Power BI template. See [Connect to Power BI](/help-center/connect-tools/powerbi).

**Data history:** Up to 2 years of historical data. The dashboard refreshes daily.

## Dashboard tabs at a glance

| Tab | What it shows |
|---|---|
| **Executive Report** | Top KPIs across sales, inventory, and advertising — weekly or monthly |
| **Traffic** | Glance views, conversion rate, and period-over-period traffic trends |
| **Sales** | Shipped revenue, shipped <Term>COGS</Term>, units, returns, and ordered revenue |
| **Advertising** | Account-level and product-level ad performance (SP + <Term>SD</Term> + <Term>SB</Term>) |
| **Inventory Snapshot** | On-hand inventory, purchase order status, and Amazon demand forecast |
| **Search** | Share of voice by brand and keyword across organic and sponsored placements |
| **Explorer** | Custom cross-metric analysis with flexible dimensions |
| **Imported Product Catalog** | Reference table of all tracked products and their attributes |

## Time periods and comparisons

Most tabs support **Weekly** and **Monthly** views. The dashboard holds up to **2 years** of history.

Comparisons use:

- **PoP (Period over Period)** — the previous week or month, depending on your view
- **YoY (Year over Year)** — the same period in the prior year
- **YTD (Year to Date)** — cumulative from January 1 to the current period

For simple metrics (e.g. revenue), changes show as a percentage. For ratio metrics (e.g. conversion), changes show in **bps** (basis points — 100 bps = 1 percentage point).

## Dashboard filters

All tabs share a common filter panel:

| Filter | What it does |
|---|---|
| **Vendor Account** | Filter to one or more vendor accounts |
| <Term>**Marketplace**</Term> | Filter to specific marketplaces |
| **Brand** | Filter by brand name |
| **Tags** | Filter to products assigned a specific tag in DataHawk |
| [**Distribution View**](/help-center/knowledge-hub/amz-vendor-account-sourcing-manufacturing) | Switch between Manufacturing and Sourcing views for inventory metrics |
| **Currency** | Choose the display currency for monetary metrics |

## For analysts & developers

**Traffic metrics**

- **Glance Views (GVs)** — Views of the product detail page when Retail is the featured seller
- **Conversion** — Shipped Units ÷ Glance Views

**Sales metrics**

- **Shipped Revenue** — Revenue at the time of shipment
- **Shipped COGS** — Price paid to the vendor × units shipped (brand's actual revenue from Amazon)
- **Shipped Units** — Units shipped to customers
- **ASP** — Average Selling Price
- **Ordered Revenue** — Revenue from all customer orders regardless of shipment status
- **Ordered Units** — All customer orders regardless of shipment status
- **Sellable OH Inventory** — Value of units available for sale (last day of period)
- **Sellable OH Inventory Units** — Number of units available for sale (last day of period)
- **Customer Returns** — Units returned, applied to the original sale date
- **Return Rate** — Returns as % of total shipped revenue

**Inventory metrics**

- **Aged 90+ Days Sellable Inventory** — Units and cost of inventory ≥ 90 days old and sellable
- **Average Vendor Lead Time Days** — Days from PO receipt to inventory arriving at a fulfillment center
- **Net Received Inventory** — Net units/cost received by Amazon after supplier returns
- **Open Purchase Order Units** — Units in confirmed but not yet received purchase orders
- **Procurable Products Out of Stock Rate** — OOS GVs on procurable ASINs ÷ Total GVs (Manufacturing view only)
- **Receive Fill Rate** — Units received ÷ Units confirmed by vendor (procurable ASINs only)
- **Sellable On Hand Inventory** — Units and cost of sellable inventory in Amazon fulfillment centers
- **Unfilled Customer Ordered Units** — Units ordered but not yet shipped
- **Unsellable On Hand Inventory** — Units in fulfillment centers classified as unsellable
- **Vendor Confirmation Rate** — Accepted PO quantity ÷ Quantity submitted by Amazon

**Advertising metrics**

- **Ad Spend** — Total advertising spend
- **Ad Sales** — Ad-attributed revenue (Same-<Term>SKU</Term> and Other-SKU)
- **Ad Sales Ratio** — Ad Sales as % of Shipped Revenue
- **ACoS** — Ad Spend ÷ Ad Sales × 100%
- **RoAS** — Ad Sales ÷ Ad Spend
- **TACoS** — Ad Spend ÷ Shipped COGS × 100%
- **CVR** — Ad Orders ÷ Clicks
- **CTR** — Clicks ÷ Impressions

**Share of Voice calculation**

- **Organic SoV** — 1 ÷ rank
- **Sponsored SoV** — (1 ÷ page) × (1 ÷ rank) × sponsored_type_factor
- Sponsored Brands receive a 1.25× factor (25% boost) to reflect their higher placement impact vs Sponsored Products

**Terminology**

- **bps** — Basis points. 100 bps = 1 percentage point
- **PoP** — Period over Period (MoM or WoW)
- **YoY** — Year over Year (same period, prior year)
- **SB / SD / SP** — Sponsored Brands / Sponsored Display / Sponsored Products
