This page provides an overview of the Amazon Brand Analytics reports available through DataHawk.

<Callout type="info">
  Brand Analytics data is sourced from Amazon Brand Analytics (ABA), through the Amazon Selling Partner API (<Term>SP-API</Term>) for registered brands.
</Callout>

## Search Catalog Performance

### Overview

Brand Catalog Performance reveals how customers engage with your brand's products during their search and shopping journey. It provides insights into key engagement metrics — impressions, clicks, cart additions, and purchases — to help you analyze your sales funnel.

**Requirements:** Brand-registered sellers or vendors with Amazon Brand Registry, with Seller account connected to DataHawk.

**Data Availability:**
- Frequency: Monthly & Weekly (typically on Mondays)
- Granularity: <Term>ASIN</Term> level
- Time Range: Most recent 7-day rolling window
- History: 12 months collected at account connection, then stored moving forward

**Data Tables:**
- `selling_partner.ba_search_catalog_performance_weekly`
- `selling_partner.ba_search_catalog_performance_monthly`

## Search Query Performance

### Overview

Search Query Performance (<Term>SQP</Term>) provides overall query performance — impressions, clicks, cart adds, and purchases — for a given <Term>ASIN</Term> and specified date range.

Use cases:
- **Optimize ad spend:** Identify high-performing queries for Sponsored Products and Sponsored Brands.
- **Boost organic visibility:** Understand which search terms drive the most conversions organically.
- **Track brand strength:** See how your brand ranks for strategic keywords relative to competitors.
- **Fix conversion leaks:** Discover drop-offs in the funnel where queries drive views but no purchases.
- **Enhance SEO:** Use high-intent search terms to improve listings and product detail pages.

<Accordions>
  <Accordion title="Examples of Insights">
    **Example 1: Funnel Drop-Off**

    Query: "wireless earbuds" — Impressions: 500K → Clicks: 4K → Purchases: 100

    Conversion rate is low (2.5%). Opportunity to improve title, images, or targeting.

    **Example 2: Brand Share Loss**

    Query: "BrandX headphones" — Impressions: 20K → Clicks: 800 → Purchases: 20 → Brand Share: 40%

    Indicates competitors are appearing for your branded terms. Consider retargeting or ad defense.

    **Example 3: High Intent Keyword**

    Query: "noise canceling bluetooth earbuds for gym" — CTR: 18%, Conversion Rate: 12%

    Signals a highly qualified audience. Should be prioritized in both SEO and paid campaigns.
  </Accordion>
</Accordions>

### Metrics Glossary

**Impressions:**

- **Total Query Impressions** — Total times a search term appeared in Amazon search results across all ASINs
- **Your ASIN Impressions** — How often your ASIN appeared for that search query
- **ASIN Impression Share** — The percentage of total impressions your ASIN received for the query

**Clicks:**

- **Total Query Clicks** — How many times products were clicked after the query across all ASINs
- **Your ASIN Clicks** — Number of clicks your ASIN received for the search term
- **Click-Through Rate (CTR)** — Percentage of impressions that resulted in a click
- **ASIN Click Share** — Percentage of clicks that went to your ASIN
- **Median Click Price (Market)** — Median price of products clicked by customers across the <Term>marketplace</Term>
- **Median Click Price (ASIN)** — Median price of your ASIN when clicked from the search query

**Cart Adds:**

- **Total Cart Adds** — Number of times any product was added to cart after the search
- **Your ASIN Cart Adds** — Number of cart adds for your ASIN
- **Cart Add Rate** — Percentage of clicks that led to a cart add
- **ASIN Cart Add Share** — Share of cart adds captured by your ASIN

**Purchases:**

- **Total Purchases** — Number of orders placed after the search query
- **Your ASIN Purchases** — Number of purchases attributed to your ASIN
- **Purchase Conversion Rate** — Percentage of clicks that resulted in a purchase
- **ASIN Purchase Share** — Share of total purchases that went to your ASIN
- **Unique Customers** — Number of unique buyers who purchased your ASIN after the query
- **Repeat Customer Rate** — Percentage of those buyers who had previously purchased your ASIN

### Requirements

- **Eligibility:** Brand-registered sellers or vendors with Amazon Brand Registry.
- **Access:** Must have connected your Seller account to DataHawk.

### Data Availability

- Frequency: Monthly & Weekly (typically on Mondays)
- Granularity: ASIN and ASIN x Term levels
- Time Range: Most recent 7-day rolling window
- History: 3 months collected at account connection, then stored moving forward

<Callout type="info">
  For now, Search Query data is only available for accounts with a catalog \<1K ASINs.
</Callout>

**Data Tables:**
- `selling_partner.ba_search_query_performance_weekly`
- `selling_partner.ba_search_query_performance_monthly`

## Search Terms

This report contains data on the most-clicked ASINs by search keyword and department for a marketplace.

**Requirements:** Brand-registered sellers or vendors with Amazon Brand Registry, Seller account connected.

**Data Table:** `selling_partner.ba_search_terms_src`

## Market Basket

This report contains data on the items that are most commonly purchased in combination with the items in the customer's basket (cart) at checkout.

**Requirements:** Brand-registered sellers or vendors with Amazon Brand Registry, Seller account connected.

**Data Table:** `selling_partner.ba_market_basket_src`

## Repeat Purchases

This report contains data on the quantity of repeated purchases of the selling partner's items.

**Requirements:** Brand-registered sellers or vendors with Amazon Brand Registry, Seller account connected.

**Data Table:** `selling_partner.ba_repeat_purchase_src`
