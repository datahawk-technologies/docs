DataHawk provides real Amazon search volume for every keyword you track, sourced from Amazon's **Search Terms Performance** report and refreshed weekly. This gives you a historical view of how demand for any keyword evolves over time across marketplaces — using actual Amazon signals rather than estimates.

## What you can use it for

- **Track keyword popularity and seasonality** — see how search demand rises and falls across weeks and months, so you can plan campaigns around peak periods
- **Identify high-volume keywords** — find the terms with the highest demand to prioritize for both organic SEO and paid advertising
- **Measure the impact of marketing initiatives** — see whether a campaign or listing change moved the needle on keyword demand
- **Support product research and market entry** — validate whether there is sufficient search demand before launching a new product or entering a new category

<Callout type="info">
  Each keyword variation is counted separately. For example, "bag" and "bags" are treated as two distinct keywords, each with their own volume. Keep this in mind when comparing keywords or summing volumes across a set.
</Callout>

## Requirements

You must have the keyword tracked in DataHawk to see its search volume data. Volume data does not backfill — it is collected from the day tracking begins.

## Supported marketplaces

| Region | Coverage |
|---|---|
| North America | All marketplaces |
| Far East | All marketplaces |
| Europe | Spain, United Kingdom, France, Netherlands, Germany, Italy, Sweden, Turkey, United Arab Emirates, India |

**Data available from:**

- Most marketplaces: 1 May 2024
- AE and SG: 15 August 2024
