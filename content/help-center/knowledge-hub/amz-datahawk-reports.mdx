The **DataHawk Reports** provide **curated, business-ready datasets** designed for reporting, analytics, and executive decision-making.

Reports in this layer are built by **combining multiple Amazon data sources** — including Seller Central reports, Amazon APIs, etc — into **standardized, trusted views**. Complex reconciliation logic, metric alignment, and data modeling are handled upstream, so the reports are ready to use as-is.

## Available reports

### Amazon Products Daily (`amzn_products_daily`)

A daily snapshot of product-level data combining metrics from multiple sources into a single unified view.
