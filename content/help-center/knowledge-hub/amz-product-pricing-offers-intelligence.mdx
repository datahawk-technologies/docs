DataHawk tracks daily product offers, prices, and featured offer (<Term>Buy Box</Term>) information for every product you monitor. This gives you a continuous, historical view of pricing movements and competitive offer dynamics on Amazon.

## What you can use it for

- **Price monitoring and alerts** — track how your prices and competitors' prices change day to day
- **<Term>Buy Box</Term> tracking** — see who holds the Buy Box, how often it rotates, and whether it is being suppressed
- **Offer-level competition analysis** — understand how many sellers are competing on a listing and how their fulfillment methods (<Term>FBA</Term> vs <Term>FBM</Term>) differ
- **Long-term trend analysis** — build a daily snapshot history of pricing and offer conditions for any product on Amazon
- **Integration into BI tools, reporting, or ML models** — data is structured for direct use in dashboards and automated workflows

## Refresh frequency

Data is delivered **daily by default**. Refresh frequency can be adjusted on demand — for example, twice a day or weekly — depending on your needs.

## Data history

History is collected **moving forward from the date tracking is activated**. There is no backfill of historical data before activation.

## Which products are covered

**Manually tracked products** — Pricing & Offers Intelligence is enabled for all products you track in DataHawk. This data is available for any product on Amazon, not just your own catalogue.

**Automatic ingestion from connected accounts** *(enabled on demand)* — DataHawk can automatically ingest active product listings from your linked Amazon accounts. Contact your DataHawk account manager to enable this.
