---
title: Self-serve Stripe subscription management in the WebApp
description: DataHawk customers with a Stripe subscription can now access and manage it directly from the WebApp settings.
date: 2026-04-08
tags: [new]
---

DataHawk customers with a Stripe subscription can now access it directly from the WebApp.

When a subscription is active, a **Manage Subscription** link appears in the **Settings** section. This link redirects directly to the Stripe portal.
