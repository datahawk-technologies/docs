---
title: Country-level Inventory Ledgers replace FC-level legacy tables
description: New country-level seller inventory ledger tables replace the FC-level legacy tables, which had an unreliable Country column.
date: 2025-11-18
tags: [new, breaking]
---

We are adding **country-level inventory ledgers**:

- `SELLING_PARTNER.SELLER_INVENTORY_LEDGER_DETAIL`
- `SELLING_PARTNER.SELLER_INVENTORY_LEDGER_SUMMARY`

These replace the old **fulfillment-center-level** ledgers:

- `RAW_INVENTORY.RAW_INVENTORY_LEDGER_DETAIL`
- `RAW_INVENTORY.RAW_INVENTORY_LEDGER_SUMMARY`

<Callout type="warn">
The FC-level ledgers are marked as legacy (not removed for new users yet). They should not be used anymore; their Country column is not reliable.
</Callout>
