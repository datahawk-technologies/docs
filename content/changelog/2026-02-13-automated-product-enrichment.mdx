---
title: Automated daily enrichment of seller-owned products
description: Daily snapshots of all seller-owned products are now collected automatically, with no manual tracking required.
date: 2026-02-13
tags: [improvement]
---

Moving forward, daily snapshots of all sellers' owned products are automatically collected, mostly surfaced in these tables:

- `PRODUCT.AMZN_CATALOG_ITEM_DETAILS`
- `PRODUCT.AMZN_PRODUCT_FEATURED_OFFER`

Previously, a product needed to be manually tracked to unlock this data.

**Scope:** enrichment runs for all paid customers' seller-owned products (checking listing, traffic, and order sources).

**Outcome:** better data coverage by default when connecting an account.
