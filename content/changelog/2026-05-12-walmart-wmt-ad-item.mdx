---
title: New Walmart Advertising dataset
description: New advertising.wmt_ad_item alias with proper sponsored_type attribution. wmt_sp_ad_item now deprecated.
date: 2026-05-12
tags: [new, breaking]
---

- New dataset `advertising.wmt_ad_item` introduced as an **alias** for `advertising.wmt_sp_ad_item`
- `advertising.wmt_sp_ad_item` is now **deprecated**
- Both datasets now correctly attribute ad item data to the corresponding `sponsored_type` (Sponsored Products / Sponsored Brands / Video), driven by campaign-level configuration

<Callout type="warn">
Please migrate to `advertising.wmt_ad_item` at your earliest convenience.
</Callout>
