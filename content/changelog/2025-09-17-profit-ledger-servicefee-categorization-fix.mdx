**Profit Ledger (Old) bug fix.**

Non-categorized ServiceFee events were categorized in Refunds > Other. They are now correctly categorized in the Others > ServiceFee category.

**Impacted tables:**

- `finance_profit`
- `finance_profit_ledger`
- `finance_profit_metrics`
