---
title: Keyword Search Volume now powered by real Amazon signals
description: SEO_KEYWORD_VOLUME_V2 now uses real Amazon search volume from the search terms performance report, with less missing data.
date: 2025-10-31
tags: [improvement]
---

Starting Monday midday, the dataset `DATAHAWK_SHARE_V2.SEO.SEO_KEYWORD_VOLUME_V2` is powered with new estimation models.

We are now directly using real Amazon Search Volume coming from a subset of keywords we can access using the **search terms performance** report.

**To be aware of:**

- Due to limited history access, the data is available **starting May 1, 2024**.
  - For `AE` and `SG`, data is available **starting August 15, 2024**.
- There is **no change in marketplace coverage** compared to the previous version.
- There is **less missing data** compared to the previous version (a smoothing is applied).

**Covered marketplaces:**

- North America: `US`, `CA`, `MX`, `BR`
- Europe: `GB`, `DE`, `FR`, `ES`, `IT`, `NL`, `SE`, `IN`, `TR`
- Far East: `AU`, `JP`, `AE`, `SG`
