---
title: Amazon All Listings Report integrated into data sharing
description: Two new tables expose Amazon's GET_MERCHANT_LISTINGS_ALL_DATA - a latest snapshot and a historical record of seller listings.
date: 2025-08-21
tags: [new]
---

Amazon's **All Listings Report** (`GET_MERCHANT_LISTINGS_ALL_DATA`) has been integrated into the data sharing.

Two new tables are now available in the `selling_partner` schema:

- `seller_inventory_all_listings`: Latest snapshot of seller listings (active + inactive).
- `seller_inventory_all_listings_history`: Full historical record of listings over time.

Both contain detailed listing attributes (<Term>SKU</Term>, <Term>ASIN</Term>, product IDs, price, quantity, condition, fulfillment channel, etc.).
