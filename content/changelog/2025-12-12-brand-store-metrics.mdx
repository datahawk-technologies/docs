- New dataset available: `ADVERTISING.amzn_brand_store_metrics`
- Provides daily brand store metrics: `sales`, `units`, `orders`, `views`, `visits`
- Includes brand entity ID (Amazon ID) and store name
- Contains 90 days of historical data; daily ingestion begins as of Dec 12, 2025
