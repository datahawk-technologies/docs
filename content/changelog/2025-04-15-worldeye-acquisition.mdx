DataHawk has been acquired by Worldeye ([press release](https://www.techdogs.com/tech-news/business-wire/datahawk-acquired-by-worldeye-technologies-to-expand-e-commerce-solutions-portfolio)), opening a new chapter of growth and opportunity.

Existing DataHawk customers will experience no disruption to their services. Account access, workflows, and points of contact will remain unchanged. The DataHawk team will remain intact under Worldeye Technologies' ownership and is dedicated to maintaining the highest level of service customers have come to expect.
