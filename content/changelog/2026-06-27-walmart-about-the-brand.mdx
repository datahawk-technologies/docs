Starting **June 27, 2026**, Walmart product data will include a new feature signal for pages where the marketing module is active: `about_the_brand`.

## What's new

When a Walmart product page has an active marketing module, DataHawk will now identify the **About the Brand** section as an activated product feature.

## Where to find it

The new signal is available in:

| Schema | Table | Column | Value |
|---|---|---|---|
| `PRODUCT` | `PRODUCT_DETAILED` | `FEATURES` | `about_the_brand` |

The `FEATURES` column lists every activated feature detected for a product, so `about_the_brand` will appear alongside other active product-page features when available.
