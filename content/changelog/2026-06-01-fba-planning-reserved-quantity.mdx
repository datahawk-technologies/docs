Several product changes shipped together.

**`seller_inventory_fba_planning`**: Four new reserved quantity columns have been added:

- `Reserved FC Transfer`
- `Reserved FC Processing`
- `Reserved Customer Order`
- `Reserved Staging`

A calculated column `reserved_including_transfer` has also been added as the sum of all four above. This exists because for some marketplaces Amazon includes FC Transfer in the `reserved_quantity` column (Amazon-IN, Amazon-SA, Amazon-AE, Amazon-BR, Amazon-AU), while for all other marketplaces it does not.

**Documentation update**: Two new pages added to give customers guidance on:

- FBA Planning dataset: Use this for FBA stock
- All Listings dataset: Use this for <Term>FBM</Term>

**Raw Inventory FBA & Raw Inventory FBA History**: `price` and `per_unit_volume` are no longer rounded.

**Market Intelligence Dashboard**: Added Rank Score (1/<Term>BSR</Term>) for easier aggregation and trend tracking.
