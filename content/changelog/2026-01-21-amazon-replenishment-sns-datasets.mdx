New Amazon Replenishment (Subscribe & Save) datasets are available.

- [Customer documentation](/help-center/data-reference/amz-replenishment)

**New models** (experimental, including 1 year of history):

- `SELLING_PARTNER.FBA_RESPLENISHMENT_SNS_PERFORMANCE`
- `SELLING_PARTNER.FBA_RESPLENISHMENT_SNS_FORECAST`

<Callout type="warn">
Old reports are no longer to be used (empty and marked obsolete):

- `SELLING_PARTNER.FBA_SNS_FORECAST_SRC`
- `SELLING_PARTNER.FBA_SNS_PERFORMANCE_SRC`
</Callout>
