- New dataset: **`reports.amzn_products_daily`**
- Combines **order-based sales**, **Amazon transaction-based revenue**, and **refunds** into a single, daily, product-level table
- Designed to align sales activity with **actual earned revenue** based on Amazon's settlement logic
