The Advertising Power BI Dashboard has been updated.

**Improvements:**

- Added Brand Store Overview & History tabs for brand-level insights
- Introduced a DSP Overview tab with DSP metrics, filters, and exploration tools
- Enabled Custom Filters in Campaign Overview & History
- Expanded campaign reporting with New-to-Brand metrics for Sponsored Brands & Display
- Added New-to-Brand DPV metrics for Sponsored Display
