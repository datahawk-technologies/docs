A new streamlined dataset is available to monitor product offers, prices, and featured offer information.

- **Main model:** `PRODUCT.AMZN_PRODUCT_FEATURED_OFFER`
- Focuses only on NEW featured offers at the moment (meaning used buybox = no buybox)
- Seller Name & Rating require an extra enrichment that will be done later
- This data relies on <Term>SP-API</Term>, so it's more reliable than its public-data tracking counterpart and more cost efficient. It can be seen as a light product tracking.

**Product Scope:**

- By default, daily for tracked products
- On demand, daily for Seller Listings (possible to enable for other product sources, like vendor products)
