---
title: Improved tax management in Profit data
description: Refined tax breakdown in Profit datasets. Tax portions extracted into separate lines with is_tax=true.
date: 2025-11-02
tags: [breaking, improvement]
---

A refined tax breakdown will be added to Profit datasets and dashboards, improving transparency in how taxes affect reported profits.

**Data Model Changes:**
- In `finance_profit_ledger` and `finance_profit`, taxes previously embedded within fees are extracted into separate tax lines, and tax portions are now marked with `is_tax = true`
- Category, subcategory hierarchies and event signatures remain unchanged
- In `finance_profit_metrics`, *Tax Liability* and *Net Profit* metrics are recalculated

<Callout type="warn">
If you apply custom computations on these metrics to account for fees taxes, make sure to remove them from your reporting.
</Callout>

**Operational Impact:**
- Reporting compatibility is fully maintained
- Reported profit may increase slightly due to exclusion of tax amounts
