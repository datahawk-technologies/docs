---
title: Amazon Advertising DSP data reliability and account visibility
description: DSP data is no longer filtered by marketplace, and connected DSP accounts are now clearly surfaced in the WebApp.
date: 2026-04-03
tags: [improvement]
---

Two improvements have been made to Amazon Advertising DSP.

- Since DSP data isn't <Term>marketplace</Term>-related, **data is no longer filtered by the marketplace of the related account.** This lets customers leverage more data than before.
- **Clearer information about connected accounts** in the Web Application:
  - More details about connected accounts (account type, account id, marketplace, advertiser name). This information is now available by clicking the account name from the accounts list; a modal opens instead of a tooltip.
  - DSP accounts connected are now surfaced (they weren't listed before). The **DSP Advertiser ID** is surfaced, in addition to the related advertising account.
  - The user that did the connection is also surfaced.
