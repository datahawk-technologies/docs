A new version of the Market Intelligence Power BI Dashboard is released.

**Improvements:**

- Added Parent/Child <Term>ASIN</Term> filters and aggregations, Price Min-Max slider, Price Bands slicer, and Top N Brands by Market Share slicer
- Expanded Market Charts tab with 5 new visuals:
  - Min/Med/Max Prices by Brand
  - Min/Max Price by Brand & Price Band
  - Market share by price band (pie)
  - Price segment share by brand (pie)
  - Stacked price segment breakdown
- Introduced 4 new metrics:
  - Est. Market Size (Category & Price Segment)
  - Est. Market Share (Total Category & Price Segment)
  - Rank Score (1/<Term>BSR</Term>) for easier aggregation and trend tracking

The documentation is updated. The lead-facing link stays the same; the report itself is updated.
