---
title: 30-minute default timeout on Snowflake Data Sharing queries
description: A new default 30-minute timeout protects customers from runaway queries that over-consume Snowflake credits.
date: 2025-11-03
tags: [breaking, maintenance]
---

A new default timeout of 30 minutes is applied to all Snowflake Data Sharing queries.

This prevents customers from consuming too many credits from erroneous queries.
