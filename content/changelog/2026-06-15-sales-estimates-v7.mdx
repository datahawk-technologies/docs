## What's improved

- **Less likely to underestimate** — model is calibrated to stay in-range or above actual sales
- **Lower average/median error** at scale
- **Abnormal spikes in history are suppressed** for cleaner trend analysis
- Estimates are now **child (variant) level** natively — aggregate to parent or brand as needed

## Data impact

On **June 15**, the full 2024+ history is replaced at once with V7 data. If you need to retain current estimate values, back them up before that date.
