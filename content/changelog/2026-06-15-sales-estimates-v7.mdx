## What's improved

- Sales estimations now consider more market signals to reflect better demand
- Upgraded our algorithm to enhance the precision of our estimates
- New algorithm allows the estimation of more products on the market than before
- Historical estimates benefit from improvements and removed abnormal spikes
- Estimates are now generated at the variant level and can be rolled up to the parent or brand

## Data impact

On **June 15, 2026**, the full 2024+ history is replaced at once with V7 data. If you need to retain current estimate values, back them up before that date.

<Callout type="warn">
