Starting **June 27, 2026**, Amazon product badge data will include a new badge signal when Amazon marks a product as **Frequently Returned**.

## What's new

DataHawk will now capture Amazon's **Frequently Returned** badge as a product badge. This helps teams identify products Amazon is flagging for higher-than-typical return behavior and track that signal over time.

## Where to find it

The new badge is available in:

| Schema | Table | Column | Value |
|---|---|---|---|
| `PRODUCT` | `PRODUCT_BADGES` | `BADGE_NAME` | `Frequently Returned` |
| `PRODUCT` | `PRODUCT_BADGES` | `BADGE_TYPE` | `Frequently Returned Badge` |

Use `BADGE_NAME` for the badge label shown on Amazon and `BADGE_TYPE` to group this signal with the corresponding badge category in product badge analysis.
