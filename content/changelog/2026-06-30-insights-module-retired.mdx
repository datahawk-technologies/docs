As of **June 30, 2026**, the DataHawk Insights module has been retired.

We are working on a stronger, more powerful module to replace it. The new experience will be designed to help teams surface meaningful changes, understand what deserves attention, and act faster from their DataHawk data.

## What changes

- The Insights module is no longer available in the DataHawk app.
- Insights documentation has been removed from the Help Center.

We will share more details about the replacement module when it is ready.
