---
title: Updated COGS date range policy
description: COGS uploads are now limited to 5 years in the past and 1 year into the future, down from 10 years of history.
date: 2025-07-22
tags: [breaking]
---

For cost optimization purposes, the COGS date range that's possible to upload has changed.

- **Before:** 10 years of history
- **Now:** possible to upload COGS data for **up to 5 years in the past and up to 1 year into the future**

Customer documentation is updated.
