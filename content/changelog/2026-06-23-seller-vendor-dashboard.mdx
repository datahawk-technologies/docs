DataHawk is releasing a new Power BI template, the **Seller + Vendor Analytics Dashboard**, designed for brands operating on both Seller Central (<Term>3P</Term>) and Vendor Central (<Term>1P</Term>) simultaneously. It consolidates traffic, sales, advertising, P&L, and inventory into a single view, with blended metrics that account for the structural differences between the two revenue models.

## What's in the dashboard

The dashboard covers 11 report pages:

- **Overview**: Combined executive summary with Total Earned Revenue, Total Ordered Revenue, blended <Term>ASP</Term>, <Term>TACoS</Term> (on both Ordered and Earned Revenue bases), and a channel revenue split
- **Sales (Seller)**: Ordered Product Sales, units, orders, ASP, AOV, page views, and conversion
- **Sales (Vendor)**: Shipped <Term>COGS</Term>, Shipped Revenue, Ordered Revenue, Ordered Units, Glance Views, and conversion
- **Advertising**: Consolidated <Term>SP</Term>, <Term>SB</Term>, and <Term>SD</Term> performance across both channels with campaign and product breakdowns
- **P&L (Seller)**: Full profit and loss from Gross Revenue to Net Profit, with waterfall chart and flexible matrix views
- **P&L (Vendor)**: Full profit and loss from Shipped Revenue to Net Profit
- **Inventory Timeline**: 30-day <Term>FBA</Term> inventory history across all fulfillment stages (Seller)
- **FBA Recommendations**: Amazon's restock suggestions and custom replenishment calculations (Seller)
- **Inventory Snapshot**: Vendor inventory health, aging, purchase orders, and Amazon demand forecast (P70/P80/P90)
- **Explorer**: Custom cross-metric analysis with any two metrics over a configurable timeline
- **Download Center**: Build and export custom data tables

## What you need

- Seller Central account connected in DataHawk
- Vendor Central account connected in DataHawk
- Advertising account (Seller and/or Vendor) connected in DataHawk
- <Term>COGS</Term> uploaded to DataHawk for accurate P&L figures
